## CSAT Script — Voice

Sir / Ma'am, I hope you are satisfied with the resolution provided to you.

_If seller says yes:_ Sir / Ma'am, after this call you will receive a survey message on your registered email ID. Request you to kindly share your valuable feedback for our conversation.

## CSAT Script — Chat

Sir / Ma'am, I hope you are satisfied with the resolution provided to you.

_If seller says yes:_ Sir / Ma'am, post this chat you will receive a survey message on your registered email ID. Request you to kindly share your valuable feedback for this chat.

---

## Rating IVR

The CSAT Survey is live. Once the call is disconnected by the advisor, the seller will receive an option to rate the IVR on a scale of 1 to 5.

| Rating | Result  |
| ------ | ------- |
| 1–2    | DSAT    |
| 3      | Neutral |
| 4–5    | CSAT    |

**Closing statement before IVR transfer:**

> I will now transfer the call to the IVR for a quick survey. Thank you for calling Shiprocket. Have a nice day.
