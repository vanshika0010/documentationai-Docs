## FCR Cases

Good morning / afternoon / evening, thank you for reaching out to Shiprocket. My name is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_. I can see that you are checking regarding _(mention query / category / sub-category)_. How can I assist you?

## NFCR Cases

Good morning / afternoon / evening. Welcome to Shiprocket. My name is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_. I can see that you have raised a concern regarding _(mention case visible in Spark)_. I would like to inform you that... _(reply with relevant information)_.

## Hold Script

May I place your call on hold for 60 seconds so that I can give you better information?

## Un-hold Script

Thank you for being on hold!

_If hold exceeds 60 seconds:_ Thank you for being on hold. I am sorry to keep you waiting for such a long time.

## Further Assistance

Is there anything else service-related I can help you with regarding Shiprocket?

## CSAT Script

If you are satisfied with the information provided, please share your valuable feedback with us on the email that you will receive in a while.

## Closing

Thank you for your time in Shiprocket. Have a nice day!
