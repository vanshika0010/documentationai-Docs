## Product Info

### KYC Types

#### Individual KYC
Complete via automated Aadhaar OTP verification, or manually upload any **two** of:
- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Passport
- Voter ID Card
- Driving License

#### Company KYC — Sole Proprietorship
Upload selfie and complete via Aadhaar or GSTIN OTP verification, or manually upload any **two** of:
- Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, Voter Card, Passport, GST Certificate, Company Incorporation Document

#### Company KYC — Registered Company
Upload selfie and complete via GSTIN OTP verification, or manually upload:
- PAN Card (mandatory)
- GST Certificate (all 3 pages) OR Company Incorporation Document

**Panel path:** KYC > Individual / Company > Upload Documents

### KYC TAT

| Type | TAT |
|---|---|
| Auto verification (OTP) | 30 seconds |
| Manual verification | 2–3 working days |

> If wrong OTP is entered 3 times during auto verification, auto verification will no longer be available. If manual KYC is rejected, seller can re-upload within 2–3 working days from rejection date.

### Bank Verification — Penny Drop

- Used when updating bank account for the first time.
- A ₹1 transaction is credited to verify the account.
- Maximum **3 attempts** via Penny Drop.
- Available on both web and mobile app.

**Path:** Settings > Company > Accounting and Bank Details

### Common Reasons for Penny Drop Failure

- Account number and re-enter fields do not match.
- Incorrect account type selected.
- Incorrect IFSC code.
- Account number or IFSC does not match the cancelled cheque.

### Manual Bank Verification (After 3 Failed Penny Drop Attempts)

Submit bank details manually: upload cancelled cheque or passbook with bank stamp and photograph.

**Path:** Settings > Company > Accounting and Bank Details > Upload cancelled cheque

TAT: 48 business hours for manual verification.

If not verified within 48 hours → fill Bank and KYC Tracker. TAT after tracker: 24 hours.

If still unverified → assign to Compliance Query. TAT: 1–2 working days.

### User Management

- Seller can create up to **14 additional users** (excluding main user).
- Not available on Lite plan.

**Add user:** Settings > User Role Management > Manage User > Add New User > Fill details > Select modules > Add User

**Change tool access:** Settings > User Role Management > Manage User > Select user > Toggle to deactivate, or Edit > Select module > Update

### Bluedart Activation — Documents Required

- Company Name
- PAN Number
- Contact Person's Number
- Pickup Address with PIN Code
- Billing Address
- Company URL / Website
- Product Category

**Panel path:** Settings > Courier > Courier Selection > Deactivated > Click Activate

If Bluedart is not activated within **6 business days** after KYC upload — assign to Compliance Queries — Ravi Jangra.

_Error screenshot is mandatory before assigning ticket to Compliance Queries._

## SOP — Account Blocked

### Scenario 1 — Reason is visible in ICRM

**Check reason:** ICRM > Seller Info > View Details > Company Details > Account Blocked Reason (top left corner)

Inform seller about the reason and assign ticket accordingly.

| Reason | Assign To |
|---|---|
| Standard block / suspicious / fraud activity | Compliance Queries — Ravi Jangra |
| Weight Fraud / Address Mismatch / Multiple Accounts | Weight Fraud Control — Sonish Panchal |

TAT: 1–2 working days.

### Scenario 2 — Reason is not visible in ICRM

Ask seller to share a screenshot of the error on email and create a ticket in your own bucket to check the image.

- Error says "Account Blocked" or "Account Blocked due to Suspicious / Fraud Activity" → assign to Compliance Queries — Ravi Jangra.
- Error shows any other message → assign to Customer Support Tech Desk. TAT: 24 working hours for callback.

## SOP — Change Company Name

Company name changes are generally **not allowed** as the company name is linked to KYC compliance.

**L1 action:** Deny the request with a soft denial and create an FCR ticket in Customer Support Call Center channel.

**If seller still disagrees (exception):**

1. Request the seller to provide:
   - Reason for the name change
   - Updated company name
   - GST document (all 3 pages)
2. Create an ACR ticket requesting these GST details.
3. Assign to **Compliance Queries — Ravi Jangra**.
4. Once details are received — change ticket status to Open in Compliance Queries channel.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Complete KYC

1. Go to **Settings > KYC**.
2. Select KYC type (Individual or Company).
3. Choose OTP-based verification or manual upload.
4. Upload required documents.
5. Submit for verification.

### Check KYC Status

1. Go to **Settings > KYC**.
2. View whether KYC is approved and active.
3. If pending, raise a support ticket.

### Add/Update Bank Account

1. Go to **Settings > Company > Accounting and Bank Details**.
2. Enter account number, account type, IFSC code.
3. A Re. 1 Penny Drop will verify the account.
4. If Penny Drop fails 3 times, upload cancelled cheque/passbook manually.

### Add Users

**Settings > User Role Management > Manage User > Add New User > Fill details > Select modules > Add User**

### Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**Settings > Password & Login Security > Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) > Toggle ON/OFF**

### Activate Bluedart

**Settings > Courier > Courier Selection > Deactivated > Click Activate next to Bluedart**

> Bluedart KYC must be completed first. Activation takes 6–7 business days after KYC approval.

### Find Your Company ID / Seller ID

**Settings > Company Setup > Company Details** — Company ID is listed here.

## Mobile App — How to Do

### Check KYC Status (Mobile)

1. Three-dots menu at bottom > **Settings**.
2. Look for **KYC** section to check status and upload documents.

### Change Primary Pickup Address (Mobile)

1. Three-dots menu at bottom > **Settings > Pickup Address**.
2. Find the address you want to set as primary.
3. Swipe left > tap **Set Primary**.

Or:
- Tap **+** to add a new address.
- Check **Set this address as Primary**.
- Tap **Verify Address**.
