## KYC

### Individual KYC

Complete via automated Aadhaar OTP verification, or manually upload any two of the following:

- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Passport
- Voter ID Card
- Driving License

### Company KYC — Sole Proprietorship

Upload selfie and complete KYC via Aadhaar or GSTIN OTP verification, or manually upload any two of:

- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Driving License
- Voter Card
- Passport
- GST Certificate
- Company Incorporation Document

### Company KYC — Registered Company

Upload selfie and complete via GSTIN OTP verification, or manually upload:

- PAN Card (mandatory)
- GST Certificate (all 3 pages), OR Company Incorporation Document

**Path:** KYC > Individual / Company > Upload Documents

### TAT

- Auto verification: 30 seconds
- Manual verification: 2–3 working days

_Note: If wrong OTP is entered 3 times in auto verification, auto verification will no longer be available. If manual KYC is rejected, seller can re-upload within 2–3 working days from rejection date._

## Account Blocked

### Scenario 1 — Reason is visible in ICRM

**Check reason:** ICRM > Seller Info > View Details > Company Details > Account Blocked Reason (top left corner)

Inform seller about the reason and assign ticket accordingly.

| Reason                                             | Assign To                                |
| -------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Standard block / suspicious / fraud activity       | Compliance Queries — Ravi Jangra         |
| Weight Fraud / Address Mismatch / Multiple Accounts| Weight Fraud Control — Sonish Panchal    |

TAT: 1–2 working days.

### Scenario 2 — Reason is not visible in ICRM

Ask seller to share a screenshot of the error on email and create ticket in your own bucket to check the image.

- Error says "Account Blocked" or "Account Blocked due to Suspicious / Fraud Activity" — assign to Compliance Queries — Ravi Jangra.
- Error shows any other message — assign to Customer Support Tech Desk. TAT: 24 working hours for callback.

## Bank Verification and Updation

### Scenario 1 — Updating Bank Account for the First Time

1. Verify via **Penny Drop** — enter account number, account type, IFSC code. A Re. 1 transaction is credited to verify.
2. Available on both web and mobile app.
3. Maximum 3 attempts via Penny Drop.

**Path:** Settings > Company > Accounting and Bank Details

### Scenario 2 — Penny Drop Fails 3 Times

Common reasons for failure:
- Account number and re-enter fields do not match.
- Incorrect account type selected.
- Incorrect IFSC code.
- Account number or IFSC does not match the cancelled cheque.

Submit bank details manually: Settings > Company > Accounting and Bank Details > Upload cancelled cheque or passbook with bank stamp and photograph.

TAT: 48 business hours for manual verification.

If not verified within 48 hours — fill Bank and KYC Tracker. TAT after tracker: 24 hours.

If still unverified — assign to Compliance Query. TAT: 1–2 working days.

## Bank Unfreeze

If seller wants to change bank details:

1. Ask the reason from the seller.
2. Inform seller: name on KYC and bank details must be the same.
3. Assign to Compliance Query — Ravi Jangra. TAT: 1–2 working days.
4. Inform seller to wait for bank details to be unfrozen, after which they can update from the panel.

## Change Company Name

Company name changes are generally **not allowed** as the company name is linked to KYC compliance.

**L1 action:** Deny the request with a soft denial and create an FCR ticket in Customer Support Call Center channel.

**If seller still disagrees (exception):**

1. Request the seller to provide:
   - Reason for the name change
   - Updated company name
   - GST document (all 3 pages)
2. Create an Awaiting Customer Response (ACR) ticket requesting these GST details.
3. Assign to **Compliance Queries — Ravi Jangra**.
4. Once details are received — change ticket status to Open in Compliance Queries channel.

## Bluedart Activation

### Verification Process

If BD KYC is completed — confirm with seller that KYC has been uploaded and ask to wait **6–7 days** for BD KYC approval.

If BD KYC is not completed — inform seller they need to complete KYC to activate.

**Documents required:**
- Company Name
- PAN Number
- Contact Person's Number
- Pickup Address with PIN Code
- Billing Address
- Company URL / Website
- Product Category

**Panel path:** Settings > Courier > Courier Selection > Deactivated > Click Activate

If Bluedart is not activated within **6 business days** after KYC upload — assign to Compliance Queries — Ravi Jangra.

_Error screenshot is mandatory before assigning ticket to Compliance Queries._
