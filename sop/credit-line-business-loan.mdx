## Product Info

### Shiprocket Instant Cash Credit Facility

A revolving pre-approved credit limit that lets sellers withdraw money whenever the business needs it. Unlike a regular loan, interest is charged only on the amount withdrawn — not the entire limit.

**Access points on panel:**
- Top Navigation Bar > **Instant Cash** tab.
- Floating widget (bottom right corner of panel).
- Apps section in the left menu.

#### Key Features

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Minimum withdrawal | Rs. 10,000 |
| Maximum active loans | 5 at a time |
| Repayment | Starts only after withdrawal |
| Limit reset | Resets as repayments are made (revolving) |
| Disbursement | 24–48 hours after drawdown request and digital agreement signing |
| Early repayment charge | 2% on principal outstanding + GST |
| Grace period (missed repayment) | 7 days; after that, interest and penal charges apply |

#### Eligibility

- Active Shiprocket seller.
- Minimum age: 21 years.
- Business operational for at least 12 months.
- Currently available for **sole proprietors only** (registered entities coming soon).

#### Repayment Method

- Primarily via **COD remittance** — a portion is auto-adjusted from Shiprocket COD payouts on scheduled dates.
- If overdue for more than 10 days: switches to **auto-debit (eNACH)** from registered bank account.

#### Important Rules

- If any loan has an overdue EMI (even by one day), you **cannot draw further loans** until the overdue is cleared.
- Rejected applications can be reapplied after **6 months**.

#### Where to View Statement

Capital Dashboard > Instant Cash section > View Statement.

### Shiprocket Business Loan (via Flexi)

A term loan product for Shiprocket sellers, managed in partnership with Flexi. The complete loan journey — from application to disbursement — is done online.

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Loan amount | Rs. 50,000 – Rs. 20,00,000 |
| Repayment tenure | 6–36 months |
| EMI calculation | Dynamic, real-time based on amount and tenure |
| Support phone | +91-9821008947 |

**Application:** Seller Panel Home > Apply Now > Validate mobile number via OTP > Customize loan amount and tenure > Complete KYC > Submit.

**If application is paused midway:** All progress is saved, including application ID, company details, and mobile number. Can be resumed later.

### CRIF Credit Score

Sellers can check their free CRIF credit score (range: 300–900) from the Shiprocket Seller Panel.

**Path:** Shiprocket Capital > Check Your Credit Score > Enter Full Name, Mobile Number, Email ID, KYC Document Type and Number > Verify OTP > View score instantly.

**Checking your own score does not affect it** (soft inquiry). Recommended to check at least once a month.

#### What Affects CRIF Score

| Factor | Weight |
|---|---|
| Repayment History | 35% |
| Credit Utilization (keep below 30%) | 30% |
| Length of Credit History | 15% |
| Credit Mix (secured + unsecured) | 10% |
| New Credit Inquiries | 10% |

### ShipSure — RTO Refund Program

Automated RTO refund program — no claims or paperwork required.

**How it works:**
- On onboarding to ShipSure, a small premium fee is charged per shipment.
- For every RTO, a flat pre-decided refund is automatically credited to the wallet.
- All RTOs are eligible (except fraudulent and very high risk orders).
- Covers both COD and Prepaid orders.
- No cap on the refund amount.

**Refund cycle:** Monthly — all eligible RTOs within a month are settled together on the **7th of the following month**.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Apply for Credit Facility

Seller Panel > Top Navigation Bar > Instant Cash tab (or floating widget bottom-right) > Apply > Fill basic details > Complete KYC > Submit > Wait for real-time review.

### Make a Drawdown (Withdraw Funds)

Capital Dashboard > Instant Cash > Select withdrawal amount > Digitally sign agreement > Funds credited to bank in 24–48 hours.

### Make a Direct Payment for Overdue Amount

Capital Dashboard > Credit Facility Dashboard > Make Payment > Alternatively use payment link from SMS reminder.

### Apply for Business Loan

Seller Panel > Home screen > Apply Now (next to Business Loan) > Validate mobile number > Adjust loan amount (Rs. 50K–20L) and tenure (6–36 months) > Submit application.

### Check CRIF Credit Score

Shiprocket Capital section > Check Your Credit Score > Enter details > Verify OTP > View score.
