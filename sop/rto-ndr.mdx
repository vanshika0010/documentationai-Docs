## NDR SOP

### System Check

1. Copy link from SPARK / Click on Internal ICRM.
2. View Details > NDR tab > Check NDR raised date and undelivered reason.
3. Check action taken by seller / buyer.
4. Check NDR communication (check Delivery Boost if enabled).

### Seller Action Path

Seller Panel > Shipments > Process NDR > Search AWB > Reattempt / RTO

TAT after BPR: 1–2 working days.

_If BPR is pending — assign case to LOPS with the **BPR Pending** tag._

### No Buyer Calls After 8:30 PM

> I understand your concern Sir / Ma'am. However, as per TRAI guidelines and our policy, buyer calls cannot be arranged after 8:30 PM to ensure privacy. But don't worry — I've escalated your concern to the courier team. Let's wait for their response by _(mention TAT)_.

### BPR Checklist

When taking BPR on call:

1. Verify buyer's name and product details.
2. Validate non-delivery (NDR) reason from buyer.
3. Confirm if FE contacted or reached for delivery attempt (share attempt date and time).
4. Confirm if buyer is still ready to accept the **packed shipment** — deny if future delivery more than 2 days or open-box delivery requested.
5. Confirm alternate mobile number and landmark.
6. Inform buyer they will receive a call from the delivery reattempt team.
7. BPR call control must stay with the advisor — limit seller interference.

### NDR CB Team Actions

1. Try BPR once. If no connectivity — raise NFCR to LOPS with **BPR Pending** tag.
2. If both seller and buyer are unreachable — raise NFCR for delivery reattempt and inform seller via email about non-connectivity.

## RTO-NDR SOP

If the RTO shipment is not delivered to the seller, the status changes to **RTO NDR**.

### Seller Action Path

Seller Panel > Orders > Orders > RTO tab > Filter: RTO NDR > Action column > Reattempt

- Seller can also change phone number and address (only for Delhivery, Ecom Express, Xpressbees).
- Seller can select reattempt date and upload call recordings.

### Key Rules

- Action must be taken within **10 days** of RTO NDR.
- If no action in 10 days — shipment is **disposed off**.
- Only **1 reattempt** is allowed. If second attempt fails — shipment is disposed off.

### Email Notifications to Seller

- When RTO NDR is raised — email sent within 4 hours (includes all AWBs).
- Day 5 — reminder email.
- Day 9 — final reminder email.

If seller wants to know why shipment got RTO — inform the NDR remarks and advise:

> Shipment will be delivered to the seller within **15 working days** from date of RTO Initiated. No further delivery attempt is possible.

## RTO Initiated — Out of TAT

If the 15 working day TAT is breached:

1. Assign to LOPS — TAT: 1–2 working days.
2. Initiate **Soft Loss Refund** from ICRM: View Details > Shipment section > Soft Loss > Select reason > Submit.

_Note: Soft loss is temporary. Amount is debited from wallet once shipment is delivered. Cannot be transferred to bank._

## RTO Escalation

Seller can escalate from the panel when:
- Shipment is in **RTO Initiated** or **RTO In Transit** status, AND
- The RTO EDD has been breached.

**Path:** Seller Panel > Orders > RTO tab > Escalate button (visible when EDD passed) > Add comment > Submit

### RTO Zone TATs (excluding RTO Initiated date and Sundays)

| Zone | Description                          |
| ---- | ------------------------------------ |
| A    | Within City                          |
| B    | Within State                         |
| C    | Metro to Metro                       |
| D    | Rest of India (excluding NE and J&K) |
| E    | North East and J&K                   |

### Key Rules

- Resolution TAT: **72 hours** from raised date.
- Re-escalation allowed within **48 hours** of escalation closure.
- Escalation auto-closes on: RTO NDR, RTO Delivered, Disposed, Destroyed, Lost, RTO Acknowledged, Put Away Done.

## NDR Verification for RTO Shipment

1. Check shipment status in ICRM.
2. Check if NDR was sent and action taken by seller.
3. Inform seller: forwarding to team; verifying attempt from buyer. TAT: 1–2 days.

_Do not raise a ticket if the issue is pending more than 10 days._
