## Product Info

### Shiprocket Credit Line

A credit facility is a pre-approved loan limit that gives sellers the flexibility to withdraw money whenever the business needs it.

**Key difference from a regular loan:** A regular business loan gives a fixed lump sum upfront and interest is charged on the full amount from day one. A credit facility only charges interest on the amount you actually withdraw.

### Shiprocket Business Loan

A financing solution for sellers registered on the Shiprocket platform, in partnership with Flexi.

**Features:**
- Loan amounts: ₹50,000 to ₹20,00,000.
- Flexible repayment tenure: 6 to 36 months.
- Dynamic EMI calculation.

### Instant COD

Sellers receive up to **70%** of COD amount the next day (S+1) after shipping.

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| Initial Remittance | Up to 70% on S+1 |
| Service Fee | 2.99% of COD amount |
| Remaining Amount | As per ongoing COD remittance plan |

**Enable:** Settings > Seller Remittance > Instant COD > Enable

> Available in Phase 1 rollout for selected **Gold and Platinum tier sellers** only.

### Credit Score

Shiprocket maintains a credit score for sellers based on shipping performance, payment history, and account behaviour. This score may affect eligibility for financial products like credit lines and business loans.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Activate Instant COD

Settings > Seller Remittance > Instant COD > Toggle ON

### Check Credit Line / Loan Eligibility

Go to **Finance > Credit** (or relevant section in the left menu) to check if your account is eligible for credit products.

## Mobile App — How to Do

### Check Finance Products (Mobile)

1. Tap three-dots menu at bottom.
2. Tap **Finance**.
3. View available financial products including Instant COD, Credit Line, and remittance details.

### Check COD Remittance (Mobile)

Three-dots menu > **COD Remittance** > Apply date filter to view remittance status.
