title: "Inbound Call Script"
description: "Standard scripts for handling inbound calls in English and Hindi"
---

## Opening

Good morning / afternoon / evening. Welcome to Shiprocket. My name is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_. How may I assist you?

**Acknowledgement (English):** Surely, I will assist you regarding your concern — _[mention seller's concern]_.

**Acknowledgement (Hindi):** Nishchint rheye, mei apki sahayta krne ki puri koshish krunga / krungi.

## Hold Script

May I place your call on hold for 60 seconds so that I can give you better information?

## Un-hold Script

Thank you for being on hold!

_If hold exceeds 60 seconds:_ I am sorry to keep you waiting for such a long time.

## Further Assistance

Is there anything else I may assist you with regarding Shiprocket?

## CSAT Script

If you are satisfied with the information provided, please share your valuable feedback over the email you will receive after this call.

## Google Review Script

If you are satisfied with the information provided, please share your valuable review on the Shiprocket website. You will receive the link after this call on your registered email ID.

## Closing

Thank you for calling Shiprocket. Have a good day.

## Hindi Scripts

**Hold:** Behtar jaankari ke liye kya mei apki call 60 seconds ke liye hold pr rkh skta / skti hu?

**Un-hold:** Hold par bane rhne ke liye dhanywaad! _(If hold exceeds 60 seconds)_ Lambe hold ke liye maafi chahunga.

**Further Assistance:** Kya mei aapki Shiprocket ke regarding kuch or sahyta kar sakta / sakti hu?

**CSAT:** Agar aap meri di gyi jankari se santusht hei, toh kripya apna valuable feedback is call ke baad bheji gyi email par de sakte hei.

**Closing:** Shiprocket mei call krne ke liye apka dhanywaad! Apka din shubh ho!
