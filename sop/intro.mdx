### Welcome to SR SOP Site

This site is your central knowledge hub for process guides, troubleshooting steps, and support procedures.

### How to find what you need

**Smart Search** — Use the Search bar at the top or press `Ctrl K` from anywhere on the site. Type a keyword — "wallet", "NDR", "KYC", "pickup failed" — and matching pages appear instantly. Best when you know the topic.

**Ask AI** — Use the Ask AI option to type your question in plain language, e.g. *"How to help with RTO without NDR"* or *"How to cancel order from ICRM"* — and get a direct answer pulled from the SOP content. Best during a live call when you need a quick answer without reading the full page. You can also check the link in the answers for the pages/sections referred to.

**Browse** — Click any topic in the left sidebar to open the full SOP. Use the "On this page" index on the right to jump to a specific section or scenario within that page.
