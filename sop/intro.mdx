This site is your central knowledge hub for process guides, troubleshooting steps, and support procedures.

### How to find what you need

#### **Smart Search**&#x20;

Use the Search bar at the top or press `Ctrl K` from anywhere on the site. Type a keyword — "wallet", "NDR", "KYC", "pickup failed" — and matching pages appear instantly. Best when you know the topic.



#### **Ask AI**&#x20;

Use the Ask AI option to type your question in plain language, e.g. *"How to help with RTO without NDR"* or *"How to cancel order from ICRM"* — and get a direct answer pulled from the SOP content.&#x20;

- Best during a live call when you need a quick answer without reading the full page.&#x20;

- Click on suggestive questions at the end of answer to check related information.

- You can also check the link in the answers for the pages/sections referred to.

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Use the **Delete** icon at the top to start a new query and get a fresh, more relevant answer without referencing chat history.
</Callout>

#### **Browse**&#x20;

Click any topic in the left sidebar to open the full SOP. Use the "On this page" index on the right to jump to a specific section or scenario within that page.
