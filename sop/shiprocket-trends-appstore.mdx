## Product Info

### Shiprocket Trends

**What is it?** A free data insights tool that shows trending products across India, which cities are buying more, and how buyers behave — helping sellers make data-driven decisions.

**Website:** https://trends.shiprocket.in/

**Who is it for?** D2C brands, e-commerce sellers, marketers, analysts, and founders.

**Data source:** Real order and shipping data from Shiprocket's pan-India network (actual buyer behavior).

**Cost:** Completely free for all users. Advanced features may be added to paid plans later.

**Access:** Google account login at trends.shiprocket.in.

**Note:** Trends shows India-wide market insights — **not your individual store performance**.

#### Available Dashboards

| Dashboard | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Home | Summarized data across orders, buyers, RTO, categories, top cities |
| Orders | Order volume and trends |
| Buyers | Buyer behavior patterns |
| RTO | RTO trends by region, category, courier |
| Categories | Product category performance |
| Geography / City Trends | City-wise buying patterns |

**Ask AI tab:** Type questions in natural language and get instant insights powered by AI.

**Update frequency:** Data refreshed regularly. Mobile browser compatible, but desktop experience is best.

### Shiprocket App Store

**What is it?** A marketplace of apps designed to enhance Shiprocket sellers' e-commerce operations. Currently features 8+ apps.

**Access:** Seller Panel > Apps (in the left sidebar) > Visit Shiprocket App Store.

**Billing:** App charges are billed through your Shiprocket wallet (one-time, monthly, or usage-based) and appear on your Shiprocket invoice and Passbook.

#### App Store Rules

- Apps review and approve all submissions for security and functionality.
- Apps can be free, have free trials, or be paid.
- Most apps auto-update; manual update notifications are sent by the developer if required.
- If an app shows "App is not available on your account," contact the app developer or KAM.
- Developers can be contacted via their listing page for support or custom features.

#### Submitting Your Own App

Develop app > Test thoroughly > Submit via Shiprocket Developer Dashboard > Must meet quality, security, and performance standards.

#### RADAR — Detailed Product Info

RADAR is an AI-powered courier intelligence tool providing real-time pincode-level visibility into courier performance.

**What RADAR tracks:**
- Courier performance ratings (scale: -2 to 5)
- SLA adherence
- First-attempt delivery success
- NDR behavior and reattempt rates
- RTO without attempt
- Timeliness of attempts after reaching hubs

**Ratings are updated dynamically** as live shipment data flows in — reflects sudden risks or pincode overloads almost instantly.

**RADAR evaluates COD, prepaid, and special shipments independently.**

**Expected improvements with RADAR:**
- Higher first-attempt delivery success
- Better NDR conversion rates
- Fewer SLA breaches
- Reduced RTOs and failed deliveries
- Improved customer experience and repeat purchases

**Best practices:**
1. Enable **Auto-Block for Stressed Couriers** from Ship Now or RADAR Dashboard.
2. Check stress indicators before bulk assignments.
3. Monitor stressed shipments and reassign high-risk AWBs.
4. Prioritize higher-rated couriers for COD, high-value, and priority deliveries.
5. Use RADAR heavily during festive/peak spikes.

RADAR does **not** automatically choose couriers — seller retains full control.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Access Shiprocket Trends

Go to https://trends.shiprocket.in/ > Log in with Google account > Browse dashboards via left-side panel.

### Switch Dashboards (Trends)

Click on dashboard names on the left-side panel: Orders, Buyers, RTO, Categories, Geography.

### Use Ask AI (Trends)

Click the **Ask AI** tab > Type your business question in natural language > Get instant insights.

### Access App Store

Seller Panel > Apps (left sidebar) > App Store.

### Install an App

Browse or search in App Store > Click app for details > Click **Add App** > Follow prompts to install and configure.

### Activate RADAR

RADAR is available directly in the Shiprocket panel. Once activated, it starts using live shipment data immediately. Check RADAR scores while assigning couriers on the Ship Now screen.

### Enable Auto-Block for Stressed Couriers (RADAR)

Ship Now screen or RADAR Dashboard > Toggle ON **Auto-Block for Stressed Couriers**.
