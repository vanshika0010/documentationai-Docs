---
title: "Outbound Call Script"
description: "Standard scripts for outbound calls in English and Hindi"
---

## Opening (English)

Good morning / afternoon / evening. My name is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_, calling from Shiprocket.

## Hold Script

May I place your call on hold for 60 seconds so that I can give you better information?

## Un-hold Script

Thank you for being on hold!

_If hold exceeds 60 seconds:_ I am sorry to keep you waiting for such a long time.

## Further Assistance

Is there anything else I may assist you regarding Shiprocket?

## CSAT Script

If you are satisfied with the information provided on the call, please share your valuable feedback with us over the email that you will receive after this call.

## Google Review Script

If you are satisfied with the information provided on the call, please share your valuable review on the Shiprocket website. You will receive the link after this call on your registered email ID.

## Closing

Thank you for your time in Shiprocket. Have a nice day!

## Hindi Scripts

**Opening:** Good morning / afternoon / evening. My name is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_, calling from Shiprocket. Nishchit rheye, mei apki sahyta krne ki puri koshish krunga / krungi.

**Hold:** Behtar jaankari ke liye kya mei apki call 60 seconds ke liye hold pr rkh skta / skti hu?

**Un-hold:** Hold pr bane rhne ke liye dhanywaad! _(If hold exceeds 60 seconds)_ Lambe hold ke liye maafi chahunga.

**Further Assistance:** Kya mei apki Shiprocket ke regarding kuch or sahyta krskta hu?

**CSAT:** Agar ap meri di gyi jankari se santusht hei, toh kripya apna valuable feedback is call ke baad bheji gyi email pr deskte hei!

**Google Review:** Agar ap meri di gyi jankari se santusht hei, toh kripya apna valuable review humari Shiprocket website pr deskte hei! Link apko is call ke baad mail ke zareeye bhejdia jayega!

**Closing:** Shiprocket mei apna keemti samay dene ke liye apka dhanywaad! Apka din shubh ho!
