## Product Info

### Brand Boost

**Launch Date:** 25th January 2023

Branded tracking experience for all SR sellers.

**Charges:** Rs. 2.99 + GST per shipment (18% GST exclusive)

**Features:**
- Brand logo display, menu links, social media links
- Instagram feed integration, YouTube product videos
- Promotional banners, custom URL, product recommendations, multiple themes

**Benefits:** Reduce "Where is my order?" queries by 65%, reduce support cost by 45%, increase repeat purchases by 15%, improve NPS by 2X.

**Billing note (from June 2023):** Charged for all delivered plus RTO delivered shipments. Charge deducted at "Pickup Scheduled" status.

**Activate:** Buyer Experience > Tracking Page > Activate Now

**Deactivate:** Buyer Experience > Tracking Page > Click here to deactivate > Select reason > Deactivate

**Features:** Multiple simultaneous campaigns, 10–15 pre-built templates or custom templates (lowercase only), target buyers by inactivity period, cart abandonment, or notification frequency.

**Charges:** Rs. 1.25 + GST per WhatsApp message. Minimum invoice: Rs. 2,000.

**TAT:** Activation and deactivation: 24 hours each — done from **team end only**, not by the seller.

### Shipment SR Secure

**Selective Secure:** Secure individual shipments valued Rs. 5,000–4,76,190. Option available during order creation.

**Blanket Secure:** Auto-secure all shipments within the Rs. 5,000–4,76,190 range.

**Path:** Settings > Shipment Features > Secure Shipment > Toggle ON

**Claim Credit:**
| Value | Credited To | TAT |
|---|---|---|
| Up to Rs. 5,000 | SR Wallet | 5–7 business days |
| Above Rs. 5,000 | Bank account | 15–20 working days (Symbo) |

**Pricing:** 0.79% of base shipment value + 0.32% convenience fee (incl. GST).

> Not available for return shipments.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Activate / Deactivate Brand Boost

- **Activate:** Buyer Experience > Tracking Page > Activate Now
- **Deactivate:** Buyer Experience > Tracking Page > Click here to deactivate > Select reason > Deactivate

### Enable Blanket SR Secure

Settings > Shipment Features > Secure Shipment > Toggle ON

### Enable Order Verification

Settings > Additional Settings > Shipment Features > Order Verification > Toggle ON

- **Verify single order:** Orders > New tab > More (...) > Mark as Verified
- **Verify bulk orders:** Orders > Select all > Bulk Actions > Verify Orders
- **Call buyer for verification:** Orders > Select order > Call Buyer > Enter contact > Initiate Call to Buyer

### Access Shiprocket App Store

From the Seller Panel, go to **App Store** in the left menu > Find AudiencePro or other VAS tools > Purchase and activate.
