## Brand Boost

**Launch Date:** 25th January 2023

Branded tracking experience for all SR sellers.

**Charges:** Rs. 2.99 + GST per shipment (18% GST exclusive)

### Features

- Brand logo display
- Menu links and social media links
- Instagram feed integration
- YouTube product videos
- Promotional banners and custom URL
- Product recommendations
- Multiple themes

### Benefits

- Reduce "Where is my order?" queries by 65%
- Reduce support cost by 45%
- Increase repeat purchases by 15%
- Improve NPS by 2X

**Billing note (from June 2023):** Charged for all delivered plus RTO delivered shipments. Charge deducted at "Pickup Scheduled" status.

### Activate

Seller Panel > Buyer Experience > Tracking Page > Activate Now

### Deactivate

Seller Panel > Buyer Experience > Tracking Page > Click here to deactivate > Select reason > Deactivate

## SR Engage / Engage+

Improves post-purchase experience for buyers via WhatsApp.

### Engage+ Key Highlights

- **Marketing:** Personalized messages, broadcast offers and products
- **Commerce:** WhatsApp shopping automation, catalogue display, product recommendations
- **Care:** 24x7 buyer support, return and refund query handling
- **RTO Suite:** Order confirmation, address rectification, COD to prepaid conversion, real-time tracking

**Activation TAT:** 24 hours. Demo TAT: 48 hours.

**Charges:** Rs. 1.25 + tax per WhatsApp message sent. Minimum invoice: Rs. 2,000 per month.

**Invoices:** Shared within the last week of the month.

## SR Convert

WhatsApp campaign and promotional messaging for SR Engage and Shopify sellers only.

### Features

- Run multiple simultaneous campaigns
- Use 10–15 pre-built templates or create custom templates (lowercase letters only)
- Target buyers by inactivity period, cart abandonment, or notification frequency

**Charges:** Rs. 1.25 + GST per WhatsApp message. Minimum invoice: Rs. 2,000.

**Activation TAT:** 24 hours. Deactivation TAT: 24 hours.

_Activation and deactivation are done from the team end only — seller cannot do this themselves._

## RTO Score

Helps sellers identify each shipment's RTO risk.

**Trial period:** Free for 30 days from date of activation.

_Note: RTO Score and settings are not visible to SR Engage sellers._

| Score   | Action                                             |
| ------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| High    | Take buyer / purchase confirmation before shipping |
| Low     | Ship any order value; no pre-confirmation required |

**Demo video path:** Seller Panel > Settings > Value Added Services > RTO Score > See Video

## RevProtect

Revenue assurance service at **Rs. 39 + GST per shipment**.

| Protection Type  | Benefit                                       |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Delayed delivery | Rs. 99 freight refund (or actual, whichever lower) |
| Delayed RTO      | 50% GMV refund, up to Rs. 499                |

Refund is processed 3 days after delivery (for freight) and 3 days after RTO initiation (for delayed orders).

## Buyer Protect

Insurance cover for buyers post-purchase against fraud, loss, or damage.

- Activated from the order tracking page (one-step activation).
- **Damaged / Fraud:** Raise claim after delivery.
- **Lost:** Raise claim 20 days after order date.

## Shipment SR Secure

### Selective Secure

Secure individual shipments valued between Rs. 5,000 and Rs. 4,76,190.

Options during manual order creation or bulk shipments.

### Blanket Secure

Auto-secure all shipments within the Rs. 5,000–4,76,190 range.

**Path:** Settings > Shipment Features > Secure Shipment > Toggle ON

### Claim Credit Details

| Shipment Value | Credited To     | TAT                       |
| -------------- | --------------- | ------------------------- |
| Up to Rs. 5,000| SR Wallet       | 5–7 business days         |
| Above Rs. 5,000| Bank account    | 15–20 working days (Symbo)|

### Insurance Pricing

- Revised premium: 0.79% of base shipment value.
- Convenience fee (incl. GST): 0.32% of net total.

_Secure facility is not available for return shipments._
