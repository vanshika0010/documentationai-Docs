## Buyer Call Handling — Inbound

If a buyer calls for shipment status or EDD — share the information. For refund, replacement, or any complaint — route to seller.

> Sir / Ma'am, you've reached the seller support helpline. We provide assistance to our sellers with their operational concerns only. We will not be able to assist you for any query related to the product you have ordered. Request you to kindly contact the seller from whom you purchased the product for further help.

_Do not share the seller's phone number with the buyer._

### Scenario Handling

| Scenario                                                       | Action                                                             |
| -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Wrong / damaged / empty / partial product complaint            | Soft denial; share seller email from ICRM; create FCR ticket       |
| Buyer refuses to contact seller                                | Raise NFCR — End User Support — Vikas Bhatia; record reason       |
| Complaint with proof (product image or unboxing video needed)  | Send ACR email; 48-hour response window; TAT 7 working days        |
| Buyer contacted seller but seller stopped responding           | Request chat screenshot or call recording; raise Open ACR          |
| Buyer approached after 72 hours of delivery                    | Soft denial; suggest contacting seller; FCR tagging                |
| Shipment status query                                          | Provide updated status from ICRM; FCR tagging                     |
| Social media threat                                            | NFCR — End User Support — Vikas Bhatia; email to SPOC             |

**Resolution TAT:** 7 working days (including Saturday).

_ACR remains active for 48 hours — inform buyer of this during the call._

### Complaint Flow (Detailed)

1. Inform buyer that investigation requires product image with visible AWB number or unboxing video.
2. Send ACR email requesting documents.
3. Once documents are shared — raise Open ACR assigned to End User Support (Vikas Bhatia).
4. Investigation team connects within 48 hours of receipt of proofs.

## SR Assured (Verified Seller)

### Buyer Dispute Flow

If a buyer contacts SR Assured support:

1. Require AWB number from buyer to identify the dispute in ICRM.
2. Initiate action for the AWB on ICRM (DNR cases).
3. Create Nugget ticket with buyer's contact details (email and number); assign to respective KAM.
4. Tag all tickets as **VF_BYR** so KAMs can identify verified buyer tickets.
5. Send first response to buyer confirming escalation and 14-day resolution TAT.

**Resolution TAT:** Maximum 14 days after buyer raises issue.

**Re-escalation allowed:** Sellers may re-escalate within 48 hours of escalation closure.

### First Response Template (Email — NFCR)

> Dear [Buyer Name],
>
> Greetings from Shiprocket. We are extremely sorry for the inconvenience you had to face. Thank you for sharing your concern — we will surely help you.
>
> We would request you to kindly share the product image / unboxing video within the next 48 hours on the same email. Once the required details are shared, the team will start the investigation and update you with the status in the next 14 days.
>
> Please note: If we do not receive a revert within the mentioned timeline, this email will get closed automatically.
>
> Thanks and Regards,
> Team Shiprocket
