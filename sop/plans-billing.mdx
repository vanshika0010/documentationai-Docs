## SAAS Plan Upgrade

**Path:** Seller Panel > Billing > Current Plan Details > Select Plan > Activate > Select duration > Pay Now

Payment options: Credit / Debit card, Net banking, UPI, Debit card + ATM PIN, 3rd party wallets.

Plan activates **instantly** on successful payment.

_Tip: Before escalating to sales team, describe Shiprocket USPs — COD and early COD plans, SRX, warehouse facility, etc._

## SAAS Plan Downgrade

### Scenario 1 — Contacted Within 30 Days of Activation

- Try to retain seller; ask for reason.
- Inform that remaining amount cannot be refunded.
- Explain the 10-day grace period — seller can use plan rates for free for additional 10 days.
- On Day 11, plan automatically migrates to Lite.

### Scenario 2 — Contacted After 30 Days or in Grace Period

- Try to retain; explain the ongoing grace period.
- If seller agrees — guide to panel path: **Seller Panel > Downgrade (blue button on the right side)** — plan is downgraded immediately.
- If seller is reluctant — assign to Finance Desk.

## Postpaid Service

Gives sellers a dynamic daily shipping limit based on COD delivered shipments — no need to maintain wallet balance for COD shipments.

**Activate:** Settings > Remittance Settings > Toggle ON for Postpaid > Upload signature > Accept agreement > Submit

_Postpaid is available on desktop version only. Limit can only be used for COD shipments. For prepaid shipments, seller must recharge wallet separately._

### Deactivation

**If no shipment is In-Transit:** Process deactivation — TAT: 24–48 hours.

**If any shipment is In-Transit:**

> Sir / Ma'am, there are some open shipments in your account. You will be able to deactivate the postpaid plan once all shipments are delivered.

## Change Label Settings

**Path:** Settings > Change Label

### Supported Changes

| Setting                    | Exceptions (cannot be hidden)                                  |
| -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Order value on label       | Bluedart, Delhivery, Wow, DotZot, Professional, Gati, Shadowfax |
| Mobile number on label     | Ekart and Bluedart                                             |
| Address on label           | Ekart and Bluedart                                             |
| Product name on label      | Available only for XpressBees and Ekart                        |

_Note: 6x4 inch label size is used for all couriers except Bluedart. Changes apply only to shipments created after the setting is updated._

## Packaging

**Path:** Seller Panel > Setup and Manage > Packaging > Buy Packaging

Steps:

1. Click **Buy Packaging** (top right).
2. Confirm redirect to packaging.shiprocket.in.
3. Place order for: corrugated boxes, plain courier bags, transparent tapes, brown tapes, or plastic stretch film.
4. Order confirmation ID sent to registered email.

**Minimum order quantity:** 100 units.

**Cancellation:** Possible before the order is shipped.

**TAT:** Check EDD on ICRM under the Shipment tab and communicate accordingly.

## Milestone Program

**Live Date:** 1st December 2025

New sellers receive discount coupons on their 2nd, 3rd, and 4th shipments.

| Shipment | Coupon Unlocked | Valid On       |
| -------- | --------------- | -------------- |
| 1st      | Welcome20       | 2nd shipment   |
| 2nd      | Delight20       | 3rd shipment   |
| 3rd      | Ship20          | 4th shipment   |

- Each coupon gives **20% off** on shipping cost, capped at Rs. 100.
- Coupons must be **manually applied** during checkout — they are not auto-applied.
- If a coupon is not used on the eligible shipment, it expires and does not roll over.
- Program is available on **web only** (app rollout coming soon).
- No activation required — automatically enabled for every new seller after first AWB assignment.
- Program is completely free.

### Key FAQs

**What counts as a "successful shipment"?**
AWB Assignment.

**Will sellers be notified?**
Yes — via WhatsApp, SMS, Email, and Seller Panel nudges. Advisors do not need to manually inform sellers unless a specific query comes in.
