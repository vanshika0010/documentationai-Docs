## SOP — Delivery Dispute

### Dispute Types

- **DNR Forward:** Shipment delivered but not received by buyer.

- **DNR RTO:** Shipment RTO delivered but not received by seller.

- **DNR Reverse / Return:** Return shipment delivered but not received by seller.

- **Wrong / Partial / Damage / Empty** delivered to buyer.

- **Wrong / Partial / Damage / Empty** delivered to seller.

- **DNU:** Shipment physically delivered but status not updated.

**WhatsApp link for proofs:**
[https://telephonycloud.co.in/portalv2/](https://telephonycloud.co.in/portalv2/)

**Path:** KartShip Rocket > Messaging > Chat > Click (+) > Enter mobile number > Select template "Dispute" > Add text in variable > Send

## SOP — POD (Proof of Delivery)

POD feature is live on ICRM for: Delhivery, Bluedart, Shadowfax, Ekart, XpressBees, and DTDC — **forward delivery only**.

- POD available **24 hours after delivery**.

- Ask seller to wait 24 hours and create FCR ticket.

- If POD not available after 24 hours — create NFCR ticket.

- If seller denies POD and raises a dispute — raise NFCR as per BAU (no change in SOP).

## Product Info — Shipcoin

| Question                        | Answer                                                                        |
| ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Where can I check my Shipcoins? | Top right icon on myShiprocket app > Click on coins for details               |
| How can I earn Shipcoins?       | 1% of order value OR 250 Shipcoins per fulfilled order (whichever is minimum) |
| What is the validity?           | 12 months from date of credit                                                 |
| How can I use Shipcoins?        | To avail gifts and rewards from leading brands via partner payment gateway    |
| When are Shipcoins credited?    | On each order delivery                                                        |
| Can I transfer Shipcoins?       | No — not transferable to any other member                                     |
| Will seller have visibility?    | No — Shipcoins are for buyers only                                            |

**Steps for buyer to use Shipcoins:**

1. Choose **Pay with Reward** during payment.

2. Enter OTP sent to device and submit.

3. Points are applied and discount is reflected on the order.

For heavy shipments in bulk — sellers get a separate panel for shipping.

**Helpline:** 9990702233 (Working hours: 10:30 AM–6:30 PM, Monday–Saturday)
