## Delivery Dispute SOP

### Dispute Types

- **DNR Forward:** Shipment delivered but not received by buyer.
- **DNR RTO:** Shipment RTO delivered but not received by seller.
- **DNR Reverse / Return:** Return shipment delivered but not received by seller.
- **Wrong / Partial / Damage / Empty** delivered to buyer.
- **Wrong / Partial / Damage / Empty** delivered to seller.
- **DNU:** Shipment physically delivered but status not updated.

**WhatsApp link for proofs:**
https://telephonycloud.co.in/portalv2/

**Path:** KartShip Rocket > Messaging > Chat > Click (+) > Enter mobile number > Select template "Dispute" > Add text in variable > Send

## POD (Proof of Delivery)

POD feature is live on ICRM for: Delhivery, Bluedart, Shadowfax, Ekart, XpressBees, and DTDC — **forward delivery only**.

- POD available **24 hours after delivery**.
- Ask seller to wait 24 hours and create FCR ticket.
- If POD not available after 24 hours — create NFCR ticket.
- If seller denies POD and raises a dispute — raise NFCR as per BAU (no change in SOP).

## Delivery Boost

AI feature that auto-initiates delivery failure messages to buyers and requests reattempt confirmation.

**Launch Date:** 22nd February 2023

- Free for sellers who joined after 22nd February 2023.

- Before 20 BPR — seller is in endless trial; no communication is sent.
- After free trial ends — seller cannot avail free service again.

| Where can I check my Shipcoins?   | Top right icon on the myShiprocket app > Click on coins for details             |

| What is the validity?             | 12 months from date of credit                                                   |
| How can I use Shipcoins?          | To avail gifts and rewards from leading brands via partner payment gateway      |
| When are Shipcoins credited?      | On each order delivery                                                          |
| Can I transfer Shipcoins?         | No — not transferable to any other member                                       |

**Steps for buyer to use Shipcoins:**

1. Choose **Pay with Reward** during payment.
2. Enter OTP sent to device and submit.
3. Points are applied and discount is reflected on the order.

## ONDC

ONDC stands for Open Network for Digital Commerce — a Government of India initiative for open e-commerce.

> We are closely integrated with ONDC. It gives you a platform to connect with multiple buyers. I am arranging a callback for you — our team will connect within 2–3 days.

**Support helpline:** 8860542050
**Support email:** supportondc@shiprocket.com

### For ONDC Onboarding

Eligible channels: Shopify, WooCommerce, Unicommerce only. Personal websites — soft denial or suggest using these channels.

**Required details:**
- Seller name and calling number
- Company ID
- Website channel

### For ONDC Deactivation

- Seller name and calling number
- Company ID
- Reason

TAT: 2–3 days.

### Remittance

D+7 days for all shipments (prepaid and COD). If TAT crossed — create NFCR ticket to ONDC Customer Support.

## Order Verification

Allows sellers to verify COD and prepaid orders before shipping.

**Enable:** Settings > Additional Settings > Shipment Features > Order Verification > Toggle ON

**To verify individual orders:**
Orders > New tab > More (...) icon > Mark as Verified

**To verify bulk orders:**
Orders > Select all > Bulk Actions > Verify Orders

**To call buyer for verification:**
Orders > Select order > Call Buyer > Enter buyer contact > Initiate Call to Buyer
