## System Check

Copy the link from SPARK and paste it in a new tab.

## Scenario 1 — Seller wants to create an order from the panel

**Path:** Seller Panel > Orders > Add Orders > New > Add Order > Domestic > Add details > Click on Add Order > Ship Now > Select Courier Partner

## Scenario 2 — Seller is unable to create an order from the panel

**Resolution:** Ask the seller for a screenshot of the panel within 48 hours of ticket creation. Assign the case to **Customer Support Tech Desk** and share a TAT of 24 hours.
