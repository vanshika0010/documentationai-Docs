## Apology Statements

- I apologize for the inconvenience that you are facing.
- Apologies for the trouble; our team is looking into it.
- I am extremely sorry for the inconvenience.
- I am extremely sorry about this situation.
- I'm sorry for the trouble that you have faced.

## Empathy Statements

- I understand that this situation is difficult, but rest assured I will find a solution for you.
- I do understand your situation. I will surely help you with this.
- I understand sir — this is wrong and this must have given an impact to the business.
- I would feel the same if this happened to me.
- Let me check what best I can do for you.

---

## Power Statements

### For NDR Desk

> Don't worry Sir / Ma'am, I will take up your issue with the courier and try to get it resolved at the earliest. We will update you on the status of the case.

### For Delivery Dispute Desk (evidence required)

> To take up the dispute with the courier, I need your assistance in providing the required _(name the requirement)_ evidence.

### General Power Lines

- I understand that this situation is difficult, but don't worry Sir / Ma'am, I will get this resolved for you.
- I do understand your situation. Let me see how I can help you here.
- I understand — this must have given an impact to the business.
- Don't worry, I will try my best to fix this for you.
- Let me check what best I can do for you — allow me once.
- I understand this is troubling you but don't worry, I am here to assist you every step of the way.
- I will put my best efforts to help you.
