## In-Transit Damage

### System Check

1. Copy link from SPARK and paste in a new tab / Click on Internal ICRM.
2. View Details > Shipment section.
3. Check shipment status.

### Scenario 1 — Seller has not shared preship images

> Sir / Ma'am, as I checked here, your shipment status is showing in-transit damage. I would like to request you to please share preship images with the shipping label. Our team will provide you a resolution within 24–48 hours. I am sharing an email on your registered email ID — kindly share the proof.

### Scenario 2 — Seller has already shared preship images

> Sir / Ma'am, as I checked here, your shipment status is showing in-transit damage and preship images have already been shared by you. I request you to please wait 24–48 hours for resolution.

_Note: If seller is unable to share images from their end, or asks about the image for damage, raise an Open ticket._

## Shipment Misroute

### System Check

1. Click on the link from SPARK for AWB details.
2. Confirm shipment details > Check status > View Details > Shipment tab > Check EDD.
3. Check SR Tracking.

### Resolution Steps

1. Check revised EDD on ICRM and inform seller.
2. If it is faster to ship a new order than wait for delivery of the misrouted shipment — ask seller to ship another order and RTO the misrouted one.
3. Provide freight refund for both shipments.
4. Raise LOPS ticket for RTO.
5. Fill the [Misrouted Shipment Tracker](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe_aw0Ugyd-FwzNSSLbNTS5G5zRU-Qz83vKed44_F15CRG77w/viewform?pli=1) — BPOC team will process refund the same day.
