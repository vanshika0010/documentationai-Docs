## System Check

1. Copy link from SPARK and paste in a new tab / Click on Internal ICRM.
2. Check the shipment status and proceed accordingly.

## Eligible Statuses for Cancellation

You can cancel an order or shipment when in any of the following statuses:

- AWB Assignment
- Pickup Scheduled
- Pickup Error
- Pickup Exception
- Label Generated
- Pickup Generated
- Pickup Rescheduled

**Path:** Seller Panel > Orders > Process Orders > Click on Order ID > Cancel

| Order Stage   | Pop-up Option                        |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Processing    | Cancel Order                         |
| Ready to Ship | Cancel Order and Cancel Shipment     |
| Manifest      | Cancel Order and Cancel Shipment     |

_Note: In "Out for Pickup" status, the cancel option is not available._

## Refund Timelines

- **Order cancellation:** Refund within 3–4 days to SR wallet (auto).
- **Shipment cancellation:** Refund within 24 working hours.

---

## Cancellation from ICRM (SSO Logins Only)

Only proceed if the seller cannot cancel from the panel or refuses reassignment.

1. Confirm shipment status is eligible (listed above).
2. Click **Cancel Pickup** in ICRM under the Shipment tab.
3. Select the cancellation reason from the dropdown.
4. Inform seller: cancellation and refund processed within **2–3 hours**, refund appears in SR wallet.
5. If cancellation requested status persists more than 3 days with no refund — click **Freight Refund** button. Inform seller refund will reflect in 2–3 hours.

## Order Cancellation Action Plan

- If cancellation status is less than 3 days old — inform seller to wait for 3–4 days from the cancellation requested date.
- If seller cannot share the date — assign to pickup group with note: _"Seller is unable to share the cancellation requested date hence assigning to pickup group."_
- If cancellation is confirmed to be more than 4 days old — mention in private note and assign to the pickup group / channel.
