## Tracking Page — Contact Information Mismatch or Update Required

### What to Check

The tracking page pulls contact details (email and phone number) directly from the seller's **Brand Boost** settings — not from their general account profile. Check and correct the details there.

**Brand Boost → Verify email and contact number**

### Seller Reply
> The contact information displayed on your tracking page is fetched from your Brand Boost settings, not your general account profile. Please update the email address and contact number in Brand Boost and the tracking page will reflect the changes.
