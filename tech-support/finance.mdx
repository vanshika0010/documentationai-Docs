## Passbook and Wallet Balance Not Matching

**Sub-category:** Wallet Balance Mismatch

### What to Check

This issue typically stems from unbilled VAS charges creating a discrepancy between the wallet database and VAS database. Run both queries and compare the results.

**Step 1 — Check the wallet DB:**
```sql
SELECT * FROM company_wallet
WHERE wallet_company_id = 406573
```

**Step 2 — Check VAS DB (common):**
```sql
SELECT sum(amount) FROM vas_transactions
WHERE company_id = 406573
AND is_billable = 1
```

If a difference exists between the two values, the issue is on the VAS side. If the values match, check Metabase to identify when the mismatch started and escalate to the Dev Team to reconcile the balance.

### Seller Reply
> The wallet balance mismatch has been fixed.

## COD Not Received

### What to Check

A pending reversal on the account will block COD release. Run the query below to check if a reversal is pending (i.e., `rcrf_id` is NULL and `status = 11`):

```sql
SELECT * FROM awbs
WHERE company_id = 2608868
AND rcrf_id IS NULL
AND status = 11
```

### Seller Reply
> Your COD remittance will be released in the next cycle.

### What to Check

Check the seller's Passbook for any cashback or coupon credits. If cashback or coupons are present on the account, the wallet balance cannot be transferred — this is expected system behaviour.

### Seller Reply
> The wallet transfer could not be completed because your account has cashback or coupon credits applied. These amounts are non-transferable.

## COD Remittance Inquiry

### What to Check

To show the seller exactly what will be released in their next remittance cycle, run the query below, replacing the `company_id` at the end with the seller's ID:

```sql
SELECT
  awbs.awb_code,
  awbs.status,
  shipments.delivered_date,
  shipments.total
FROM awbs
INNER JOIN shipments ON shipments.awb = awbs.awb_code
INNER JOIN plan_metadata ON plan_metadata.company_id = shipments.company_id
INNER JOIN remittance_setting
  ON remittance_setting.company_id = shipments.company_id
  AND remittance_setting.status = 1
WHERE awbs.status IN (0, 1)
  AND plan_metadata.plan_id != 18
  AND shipments.sr_courier_id NOT IN (49, 28, 71, 74)
  AND awbs.assigned_date_time > CURRENT_DATE - INTERVAL 3 MONTH
  AND shipments.delivered_date IS NOT NULL
  AND awbs.cod = 1
  AND (awbs.api_delivery_status = 1 OR awbs.mis_delivered = 1)
  AND shipments.delivered_date >= '2022-04-01 00:00:00'
  AND shipments.status = 7
  AND awbs.company_id = 2942770;
```

### Seller Reply
> The COD remittance amount shown will be released in your next cycle.

## COD Remittance Not Received

### What to Check

If an `rto_initiated_date` is present in the shipments table, it may be blocking the remittance release. Remove the date using the query below, then confirm the remittance processes in the next cycle:

```sql
UPDATE shipments
SET rto_initiated_date = NULL,
    rto_delivered_date = NULL
WHERE shipments.id = 1002273991
LIMIT 1;
```

### Seller Reply
> Your COD remittance will be released in the next cycle.

## No GSTIN in Invoice

**Category:** Tech — Pre Assignment
**Sub-category:** Invoice Issue

### What to Check

Verify that the seller's GSTIN is updated in their account. Navigate to:

**Settings → Company Setup → Billing, Invoice & GSTIN**

If the GSTIN is missing, the seller needs to add it. Once added, it will automatically appear on all future invoices. It will not be applied retroactively to past invoices.

[Update GSTIN Settings](https://app.shiprocket.in/sellers/settings/company-setup/billing-invoice-gst)

### Seller Reply
> Please update your GSTIN in your Shiprocket account for it to appear on invoices.
>
> Go to **Settings → Company Setup → Billing, Invoice & GSTIN**, enter your GSTIN, and it will be reflected on all future invoices.
>
> [Update GSTIN](https://app.shiprocket.in/sellers/settings/company-setup/billing-invoice-gst)
