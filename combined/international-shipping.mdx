## SOP — International Shipping Activation

**Step 1:** Seller Panel > Settings > International

**Step 2:** Complete International KYC from the seller panel.

### Documents Required for International KYC

- **IEC (Import Export Code):** Obtain from DGFT — https://www.dgft.gov.in/CP/
- **AD Code (Authorized Distributor Code):** Get from bank for Delhi Port.
- **Company/Individual KYC:** PAN, Aadhaar, GSTIN, etc.

**KYC TAT:** International facility activated within **48 working hours** after KYC completion.

## SOP — International Delivery Delay

1. Copy link from SPARK / Click on Internal ICRM.
2. View Details > Shipment section > Check EDD and Tracking.
3. Create NFCR ticket to LOPS team. TAT: 1–2 working days.
4. If seller is not satisfied — transfer call to international desk or arrange callback. TAT: 4–5 working hours.
5. Fill the tracker: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1qQqK4kEc1qniUfL71bqFZgabcmEmHTk82zjT2WuXFKk/viewform?edit_requested=true

## SOP — New Seller Interested in International Shipping

If a new seller is interested, fill the Pipedrive form with:
- Name, country code and number, email, address
- Product category
- Planned start date for shipments
- Platform currently receiving orders from
- Company website
- Expected monthly shipments
- Whether they also do international shipments
- Current shipping partner
- Source: Call Center

Inform seller: Sales team will call back within **48 hours**.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Activate International Shipping

1. Go to **Settings > International**.
2. Complete International KYC:
   - Upload IEC (Import Export Code) — obtain from DGFT.
   - Upload AD Code — obtain from your bank for Delhi Port.
   - Upload Company/Individual KYC documents.
3. Wait 48 working hours for activation.

### Check Shipping Rates (International)

**Tools > Rate Calculator** — select international origin/destination to view applicable rates and service options.

### Create an International Order

Follow the standard order creation flow. International courier options will appear if the delivery pincode/country is serviceable and international KYC is complete.

---

## Mobile App — How to Do

### Create an International Shipment (Mobile)

1. Tap **Add Orders** from the bottom menu.
2. Select the appropriate order type and enter an international delivery address.
3. International courier options will appear based on serviceability.
4. Assign courier and schedule pickup.

> International KYC and activation must be completed on the **web panel** first.
