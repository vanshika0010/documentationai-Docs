### Amazon Shipping (1 kg, 2 kg, 5 kg)

**Eligibility:**
- GSTIN invoicing enabled in Shiprocket settings.
- Prepaid orders only (COD coming soon).
- Order value below ₹50,000.
- Dimensions: max 70×70×45 cm.
- Weight: min 0.5 kg, max 18 kg.
- Non-hazmat items only.
- Surface mode; thermal printing labels required.
- Cannot make changes to Amazon label (including hiding SKU name).

**Pickup Slots:**
| Shipment Created | Pickup Window |
|---|---|
| Before 9:45 AM | 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM |
| Before 12:45 PM | 1:00 PM – 4:00 PM |

Pickups are **auto-scheduled** — seller does not need to generate pickup manually.

### Multi-Packet Shipment (MPS)

- Available for **Delhivery only**.
- Manual orders only — does not work for channel integration orders.
- Max: 20 packets (1 Master + 19 Child AWBs).
- Max combined weight: 1,000 kg.
- Minimum chargeable weight: 10 kg.
- Pickup: Next day only.

**Activation:** Contact support (9711941080) or KAM. After activation, seller can enter package count > 1 during order creation.

**MPS Training:** Advisors must schedule training using the booking link at [Calendly](https://calendly.com/d/cs9c-7kk-m2s). After training, activation request is raised by the trainer to LOPS.

### Split Shipment

Allows partial fulfillment based on available stock.

**Enable:** Settings > Shipment Features > Split Shipment > Toggle ON

Works for **manual orders only**.

**Steps:**
1. Create order manually via Add Order.
2. Go to Processing tab > Select order > Ship Now.
3. Select products to ship in the popup.
4. Update package weight.
5. Click **Split and Ship Now**.
6. Choose courier partner.
7. Redirected to Processing tab for remaining partial shipment.

### Hyperlocal Shipping (Shiprocket Quick)

- Same-day pickup and delivery within **40–50 km** range.
- Simply create a shipment manually — hyperlocal couriers appear automatically based on address and PIN code.

- Couriers: Porter, Borzo, Flash, Zypp, Loadshare Hyperlocal.

For queries on Shiprocket Quick — arrange callback from sales team. SPOC: Dipu Kumar Mandal. Callback TAT: 30 minutes.

### RADAR — AI Courier Intelligence Tool

RADAR gives sellers real-time visibility into courier performance and last-mile network health at a pincode level.

**What RADAR tracks:**
- Courier performance ratings (scale: –2 to 5)
- SLA adherence
- First-attempt delivery success
- NDR behaviour and reattempt rates
- RTO without attempt
- Timeliness of attempts after reaching hubs

**How to use RADAR:**
- View RADAR scores while assigning couriers on the Ship Now screen.
- Open the RADAR Dashboard for deeper pincode-level insights.
- Enable **Auto-Block for Stressed Couriers** to automatically avoid stressed couriers during assignment.

**Best Practices:**
1. Enable Auto-Block for Stressed Couriers from Ship Now or RADAR Dashboard.
2. Check stress indicators before bulk assignments.
3. Monitor shipments tagged with stress indicators and reassign high-risk AWBs.
4. Prioritize higher-rated couriers for COD, high-value, and priority deliveries.
5. Use RADAR heavily during peak sales/festive spikes.

RADAR does not automatically choose couriers — seller retains full control.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Enable Split Shipment

**Settings > Shipment Features > Split Shipment > Toggle ON**

### Create a Split Shipment Order

1. Create order manually via Add Order.
2. Go to **Processing tab** > Select order > **Ship Now**.
3. Select specific products to ship in the popup.
4. Update package weight.
5. Click **Split and Ship Now**.
6. Choose courier partner.
7. Return to Processing tab for remaining partial shipment.

### Use the Rate Calculator

**Tools > Rate Calculator** — check freight rates for any pickup/delivery pincode before order creation.

### Block/Unblock ODA or RTO Pincodes

1. Go to **Settings > Courier Selection**.
2. Click on the courier for which you want to manage pincodes.
3. Click **Block Pincodes**.
4. Select option: Courier (all ODA/RTO pincodes), Account (account-specific), or Custom (manual entry).
5. Click **Submit**.

Follow the same steps to **unblock** pincodes.

### Upload E-Way Bill (Forward Orders)

**At Pickup Generation:** Orders > Schedule Pickup popup > Upload E-Way Bill number, PDF, and invoice.

**From Ready to Ship Screen:** Orders > Ready to Ship > Upload Eway Bill button.

**For RTO Shipments:** Orders > Shipments > RTO > Upload Eway Bill.

> E-Way Bill validity: 7 days. Required for shipments with value over ₹50,000.

### Activate RADAR

RADAR is available directly within the Shiprocket panel. Once activated, it starts using live shipment data immediately.

## Additional Product Info

### Shipping Zones

Courier companies divide all serviceable pincodes into shipping zones that determine freight charges and delivery time:

| Zone | Definition |
|---|---|
| Zone A (Within City) | Pickup and delivery pincodes are in the same city |
| Zone B (Within State) | Pickup and delivery pincodes are in the same state/region |
| Zone C (Metro to Metro) | Between major metro cities |
| Zone D (Rest of India) | All other pincodes excluding NE and J&K |
| Zone E (NE and J&K) | North East India and Jammu & Kashmir |

### Courier Partners on Shiprocket

Delhivery, Blue Dart, Shadowfax, Xpressbees, Ecom Express, DTDC, Ekart, India Post, Amazon Shipping, Pikndel, and many more.

All couriers can be activated by the seller except Bluedart, which requires KYC verification.

### Weight Classes

Couriers are categorized by weight slabs: 0.5 kg, 1 kg, 2 kg, 5 kg, and 10 kg.

### Courier Priority Options

| Priority | What it does |
|---|---|
| Recommended by Shiprocket | SR decides the best courier based on your shipping needs |
| Cheapest | Lists couriers by lowest freight cost |
| Fastest | Lists couriers by earliest EDD |
| Best Rated | Lists couriers by performance rating |
| Custom | Seller-defined priority order |

### Direct Ship Feature

Automatically assigns courier based on courier priority without showing the selection screen. Pickup is scheduled automatically for the next day and labels are downloaded automatically.

### Courier Rules — Conditions Available

Courier rules can be created based on:
1. Payment Mode
2. Weight
3. Zone Wise
4. Order Value
5. State
6. Pincode
7. City
8. Dangerous Goods
9. Product SKU
10. AWB Assigned Time
11. Order Tags
12. TAT and TAT Compliance %
13. NDR Conversion
14. Product Category
15. Pickup Location ID

Up to **200 courier rules** can be created. Rules cannot be edited — archive and recreate if changes are needed.

**If no rule applies:** Default courier priority setting is used (Recommended by Shiprocket).

### SDD and NDD Couriers

Same-Day Delivery (SDD) and Next-Day Delivery (NDD) couriers are available for selected sellers. To find them: Setup & Manage > Courier > Courier Selection > Search "SDD" or "NDD".

### Courier Activity Logs

Setup & Manage > Courier > Courier Activity Logs — view all courier-level actions.

### LM (Last Mile) Labels

**Individual LM Label:** Orders > All Orders tab > Search shipment > Action icon > Download LM Label.

**Bulk LM Labels:** Tools > Bulk Actions > Select Tracking (AWB) Numbers > Enter AWBs > Submit > Tools > Activity Logs > Bulk LM Download > Download.

### Amazon Courier Notes

- Amazon courier cannot be used for orders from Amazon channels.
- Amazon label format is thermal and cannot be edited.
- OTP verification applies to Amazon prepaid orders over Rs. 500.

### Template Pricing

If template pricing is enabled, courier assignment is handled by Shiprocket — seller cannot manually select a courier.

### API Courier Selection

You can specify a courier by passing the courier ID in the AWB assignment API call. Courier is assigned only if serviceability is available.

### Couriers Available in Lite Plan

All prominent and best-performing courier partners are available. Bluedart requires a 30-second KYC (PAN details). For same-day/next-day delivery or document shipping, contact KAM or support.

### Label for Document Shipping

A smaller label is used for document shipments. Standard India label size: default 6×4 inch. Customize via Settings > Company Setup > Label Settings.

### Order Barcode on Label

Mandatory for all orders shipped within India using non-hyperlocal couriers. The barcode prevents handling issues at courier hubs.

### Pincode Serviceability Check

Tools > Rate Calculator > Enter pickup and delivery pincodes, weight, payment type > View serviceable couriers.

**Download serviceable pincodes list:** Tools > Pincode Mapping Zone > Enter Pickup Pincode > Export (sent to registered email).
