## Product Info

### What is Shiprocket Cargo?

SR Cargo by Shiprocket offers B2B cargo shipping solutions for heavy and bulk shipments across India. It features multiple carriers, real-time tracking, AI-driven courier recommendations, and appointment-based deliveries.

**Website:** https://cargo.shiprocket.in/

**Support:** cargosupport@shiprocket.com | 9990702233 (10:30 AM–6:30 PM, Mon–Sat)

**Login:** From cargo.shiprocket.in OR Shiprocket panel > 9-dots icon (top right) > Select Cargo.

### Key Features

- User-friendly dashboard with analytics
- Real-time shipment tracking
- Multi-carrier support
- Coverage: 24,000+ pincodes
- AI-based courier recommendations
- Appointment-based delivery (for marketplace deliveries to Flipkart, Amazon, Nykaa, Blinkit, Zepto, Jio Mart)
- Email/SMS communication and channel integrations

### Pickup Timing

- If pickup assigned **before 12 PM** — pickup attempted same day.
- If assigned **after 12 PM** — pickup on next working day.

### Couriers Used

Spoton, Delhivery, XpressBees.

### Prohibited Items

Hazardous materials, perishable goods, precious items (gold, silver), and prohibited goods (liquor, tobacco, narcotics, betel nuts, ammunition, etc.).

### Weight and Charges

- **Dead Weight:** Actual weight on scale.
- **Volumetric Weight:** L × W × H / Volumetric Factor (varies by contract).
- **Charged Weight:** Higher of dead or volumetric weight.

### Auto-Accept Rule (Weight Discrepancy)

Same as Shiprocket main panel — discrepancies auto-accepted after **7 days** if no action taken.

### Billing and Invoices

- Invoices issued on the **7th of every month** for all shipments shipped and delivered the previous month (30-day billing cycle).
- Invoices shared via email and available on dashboard.
- For invoice disputes, contact SR Cargo within 7 working days of receipt.

### Wallet

- Recharge via payment gateway (UPI, Cards, Net Banking, Pay Later). Minimum: Rs. 500.
- Or transfer balance from Shiprocket main wallet to SR Cargo wallet.

### Cargo Secure (Insurance)

Sellers covered against damaged or lost packages. Claim possible when shipment is marked lost or damaged during transit (Forward or RTO).

**Activate:** Settings > Scroll to Value Added Services > Auto-Secure Shipments (or directly in shipment creation).

### Appointments

Appointment-based delivery allows scheduling specific time slots for delivery — used for marketplace deliveries to Flipkart, Amazon, Nykaa, Blinkit, Zepto, Jio Mart.

To enable: Mark Appointment flag as True in the shipment booking journey.

### Dashboard Sections

1. **Dashboard** — Key metrics and wallet balance
2. **Shipments** — Create, track, and manage shipments; manage appointments
3. **Warehouse** — Create and manage pickup locations
4. **Weight Discrepancy** — Manage discrepancies and raise disputes
5. **NDR** — Manage failed delivery attempts
6. **Billing** — Wallet transactions, invoices, remittances
7. **Setup and Manage** — Couriers and rules

### Quick Actions (Top Right)

Rate Calculator, Create a Shipment, Track Shipment, View Passbook.

## How to Do on Cargo Panel

### Register on Cargo

1. Visit https://cargo.shiprocket.in/
2. Click **Try for free**.
3. Fill registration form (email and contact details).
4. Complete OTP verification.
5. Set up account details (billing address and KYC).

### Complete Cargo KYC

KYC via automated Aadhaar or GSTIN OTP, or manually upload:
- Selfie/photograph
- PAN Card scan
- Aadhaar Card scan
- GST Certificate (Page 1 & 3) scan
- Cancelled cheque / front page of passbook

**Important:** Address proof and billing address must match.

### Create a Shipment

Shipments > Create Shipment > Fill sender/receiver info and package details (boxes, weight, dimensions) with invoice details > Select courier > Enter E-Way Bill number (if invoice value over Rs. 50,000) > Create > Download label PDF > Stick label on boxes > Handover to pickup partner.

### Track a Shipment

Shipments > All Shipments > Enter tracking number in search bar > Click Track.

### View Payments and Transaction Logs

Billing > Passbook > Filter by tracking number > View waybill-level transactions.

### Calculate Shipping Rates

Tools > Shipment Rate Calculator > Enter weight, dimensions, origin and destination pincode > View rates.

### Check Serviceability

Tools > Serviceability > Enter origin and destination pincodes > View available couriers.

### Find Pickup ID

Shipments > Pickup Requests > View unique pickup IDs against scheduled pickups.

### Download Shipping Labels

Shipments > All Shipments > Search AWB > Click Download Label > PDF format.

### Raise a Weight Dispute

Weight Discrepancies section > Select shipment > Click Raise Weight Dispute > Provide necessary details > Submit.

### Set Courier Rules

Setup and Manage > Courier Rules > Choose: Cheapest, or Custom (define priority by weight, pincode, zone, invoice value, appointment) > Save.

### Recharge Cargo Wallet

Billing section > Recharge > Select payment method (UPI, Cards, Net Banking).

Or: Transfer from Shiprocket main wallet to Cargo wallet.
