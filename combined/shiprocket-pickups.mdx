## Product Info

### What is Pickups by Shiprocket?

Pickups by Shiprocket is a first-mile pickup service where Shiprocket manages the pickup of shipments from your warehouse or seller address. After pickup, the selected courier partner handles mid-mile and last-mile delivery to the buyer.

### Key Benefits

- Guaranteed pickup commitment within the promised timeline.
- Single pickup window — no need to coordinate with multiple courier riders.
- Courier-agnostic collection — one Shiprocket rider can collect shipments across different courier partners in a single pickup.
- Better operational reliability for long-tail and small sellers.
- Reduced pickup delays.

### How It Works

1. Shiprocket rider arrives at seller's warehouse during the scheduled pickup window.
2. One rider collects all shipments regardless of which courier partner each is assigned to.
3. After pickup, shipments are handed over to respective courier partners.
4. Courier partners handle sorting, transportation, and last-mile delivery.
5. Tracking remains active as per the selected courier.

### Availability

- Currently available in **selected active pincodes** — not all pincodes.
- Eligibility depends on warehouse location and operational coverage in the area.
- Warehouse address must have accurate location details including precise location mapping.

### Courier Partner Selection

You can continue selecting your preferred courier partner on the Shiprocket panel. Pickups by Shiprocket only changes **who performs the pickup** — not the courier handling delivery.

### Buyer Experience

No change from buyer's perspective. Delivery experience remains the same since the courier partner handles mid-mile and last-mile delivery. Tracking and updates continue as per the selected courier.

### What If Shipments Are Not Ready?

- Pickup may be marked as failed or partial.
- Repeated failed pickups may impact service reliability.
- Sellers are advised to keep shipments ready within the scheduled window.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Enable Pickups by Shiprocket

If your pincode is eligible:
1. The option will appear in your Shiprocket panel automatically.
2. You may be auto-enabled based on operational rollout.
3. Ensure your warehouse address has accurate location details (including precise GPS mapping).

### Schedule Pickup (Same Process as Standard)

1. Go to **Orders > Ready to Ship tab**.
2. Click **Schedule Pickup / Request Pickup**.
3. Select pickup date.
4. If eligible, Shiprocket's own fleet will perform the pickup instead of the courier's rider.

> The pickup scheduling process remains the same regardless of whether Pickups by Shiprocket or the courier's own fleet performs the pickup.
