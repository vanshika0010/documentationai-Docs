## Product Info

### Eligible Statuses for Cancellation

An order or shipment can be cancelled when in any of the following statuses:

- AWB Assignment
- Pickup Scheduled
- Pickup Error
- Pickup Exception
- Label Generated
- Pickup Generated
- Pickup Rescheduled

> **Important:** Orders **cannot** be cancelled once they reach **"Out for Pickup"** status.

### Refund Timelines

| Cancellation Type | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| Order cancellation | 3–4 days to SR wallet (auto) |
| Shipment cancellation | Within 24 working hours |

### Pop-up Options by Stage

| Order Stage | Cancel Pop-up Option |
|---|---|
| Processing | Cancel Order |
| Ready to Ship | Cancel Order and Cancel Shipment |
| Manifest | Cancel Order and Cancel Shipment |

## SOP — Order Cancellation

### System Check

1. Copy link from SPARK and paste in a new tab / Click on Internal ICRM.
2. Check the shipment status and proceed accordingly.

### Cancellation from Seller Panel

**Path:** Seller Panel > Orders > Process Orders > Click on Order ID > Cancel

### Cancellation from ICRM (SSO Logins Only)

Only proceed if the seller cannot cancel from the panel or refuses courier reassignment.

1. Confirm shipment status is eligible (listed above).
2. Click **Cancel Pickup** in ICRM under the Shipment tab.
3. Select the cancellation reason from the dropdown.
4. Inform seller: cancellation and refund will be processed within **2–3 hours**; refund appears in SR wallet.
5. If cancellation status persists more than 3 days with no refund — click **Freight Refund** button. Inform seller refund will reflect in 2–3 hours.

### Order Cancellation Action Plan

| Scenario | Action |
|---|---|
| Cancellation status is less than 3 days old | Ask seller to wait 3–4 days from the cancellation requested date |
| Seller cannot share the cancellation date | Assign to pickup group with note: "Seller unable to share cancellation requested date" |
| Cancellation confirmed to be more than 4 days old | Mention in private note and assign to the pickup group / channel |

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Cancel a Single Order

1. Go to **Orders** from the left menu.
2. Find the order using the search bar (AWB or Order ID).
3. Click on the **More (...) icon** under Action.
4. Select **Cancel Order**.
5. Confirm cancellation in the pop-up.

### Cancel Orders in Bulk

1. Go to **Orders > New tab**.
2. Select the orders you want to cancel.
3. Click **Select Bulk Action > Cancel Orders**.
4. Confirm by clicking **Yes, Cancel**.

### Check Cancellation Status

- After cancellation, the order appears in the **New** tab with a "Cancellation Requested" status.
- Refund to SR wallet is auto-processed within 3–4 days for order cancellations and within 24 working hours for shipment cancellations.

## Mobile App — How to Do

### Cancel an Order (Mobile)

1. Go to **Orders** from the bottom navigation.
2. Find your order under the relevant tab (New / Ready to Ship).
3. Tap on the **three-dots menu (...)** for the order.
4. Select **Cancel Order**.
5. Confirm cancellation.

> **Note:** You cannot cancel a shipment once it has been handed over to the courier or is in "Out for Pickup" status.
