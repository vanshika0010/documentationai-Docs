## Product Info

### Shiprocket Plans

| Plan | Monthly Shipments | Price | Channel Integrations | Rates from (excl. GST) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| LITE | Up to 60 | Free | — | ₹26.27 / 0.5 kg |
| BASIC | 60–100 | ₹950/month | 2 | ₹24.58 / 0.5 kg |
| ADVANCED | 100–300 | ₹1,800/month | 7 | ₹22.88 / 0.5 kg |
| PRO | 300–1,000 | ₹2,700/month | 7 + 25,000 SKUs + KAM | ₹20.34 / 0.5 kg |
| ENTERPRISE | 2,500+ | Contact sales | Custom | Custom |

- Plans activate **instantly** on successful payment.
- Payment options: Credit/Debit card, Net Banking, UPI, Debit card + ATM PIN, 3rd party wallets.

### ₹99 / ₹199 Business Plan (GST-Verified Sellers)

- Gives up to 10% off on shipping rates.
- ₹99/month subscription paid automatically through UPI AutoPay.
- Available on Ship Now screen: **Upgrade & Get Discounted Price**.

### Plan Downgrade

- Seller cannot get a refund for remaining plan days.
- **10-day grace period:** After plan expiry, seller can use plan rates for free for 10 additional days.
- On Day 11, plan automatically migrates to Lite.

### Postpaid Service

- Provides a dynamic daily shipping limit based on COD delivered shipments.
- No need to maintain wallet balance for COD shipments.
- Limit can only be used for **COD shipments**. Prepaid shipments require wallet balance separately.
- Available on **desktop version only**.

**Activate:** Settings > Remittance Settings > Toggle ON for Postpaid > Upload signature > Accept agreement > Submit

**Deactivation conditions:**
- If no shipments are In-Transit: process deactivation in 24–48 hours.
- If any shipments are In-Transit: cannot deactivate until all shipments are delivered.

### Milestone Program (Live from 1st December 2025)

New sellers receive discount coupons on their 2nd, 3rd, and 4th shipments.

| Shipment | Coupon Unlocked | Valid On |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | Welcome20 | 2nd shipment |
| 2nd | Delight20 | 3rd shipment |
| 3rd | Ship20 | 4th shipment |

- Each coupon gives **20% off** on shipping cost, capped at ₹100.
- Coupons must be **manually applied** during checkout — not auto-applied.
- If unused on the eligible shipment, it expires and does not roll over.
- Available on **web only** (app rollout pending).
- Automatically enabled for every new seller after first AWB assignment. No activation required.
- **What counts as a successful shipment:** AWB Assignment.

### SHIP500 Coupon Code

Gives ₹500 cashback on a minimum recharge of ₹500.

### Label Settings

**Path:** Settings > Change Label

| Setting | Exceptions (cannot be hidden) |
|---|---|
| Order value on label | Bluedart, Delhivery, Wow, DotZot, Professional, Gati, Shadowfax |
| Mobile number on label | Ekart and Bluedart |
| Address on label | Ekart and Bluedart |
| Product name on label | Available only for XpressBees and Ekart |

> 6×4 inch label size is used for all couriers except Bluedart. Changes apply only to shipments created **after** the setting is updated.

Label types: 3 types available. Contact support for details on label type preferences.

### Packaging

Sellers can order packaging materials from the panel:
- Corrugated boxes, plain courier bags, transparent tapes, brown tapes, plastic stretch film.
- Minimum order: **100 units**.
- Order confirmation ID is sent to registered email.
- Cancellation is possible before the order is shipped.

## SOP — SAAS Plan Downgrade

### Scenario 1 — Contacted Within 30 Days of Activation

- Try to retain seller; ask for reason.
- Inform that remaining amount **cannot be refunded**.
- Explain the 10-day grace period — seller can use plan rates free for additional 10 days.
- On Day 11, plan automatically migrates to Lite.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Upgrade Plan

1. Go to **Billing > Current Plan Details** or click the **Profile icon** (top right) > **Current Plan**.
2. Compare plans and select the desired plan.
3. Click **Activate**.
4. Select plan duration.
5. Apply coupon code if available.
6. Click **Pay Now** and complete payment.

### Downgrade Plan

**Seller Panel > Downgrade button (blue, right side of Billing page)**

> Only available after 30 days from plan activation. Contact Finance Desk if unavailable.

### Activate Postpaid

**Settings > Remittance Settings > Toggle ON for Postpaid > Upload signature > Accept agreement > Submit**

### Buy Packaging

1. Go to **Seller Panel > Setup and Manage > Packaging > Buy Packaging**.
2. Click **Buy Packaging** (top right).
3. Confirm redirect to packaging.shiprocket.in.
4. Select packaging type, quantity (min 100), and place order.
5. Order confirmation ID sent to registered email.

### Customize Label Settings

**Settings > Company Setup > Labels**

- Preview the label.
- Select format (A4, 4×6, etc.).
- Customize visible fields (seller address, brand name, etc.).
- Click **Save**.

### Enable State-Level Invoicing

**Settings > Company Setup > Billing-Invoice-GST > Select state > Add GST number**

> State-level invoicing provides a single consolidated invoice per state instead of per pickup address.

### Check Current Plan

**Billing > Current Plan Details** (header menu) — view active plan and compare plans.
