## Product Info

### Wallet Basics

- **Minimum recharge amount:** ₹500 (effective 14th July 2022; previously ₹200).
- Payment options accepted: Credit/Debit card, Net Banking, UPI, 3rd party wallets, Smart Collect.
- Coupon value in wallet is **not transferred** to bank — only wallet amount is transferred.

### Convenience Fee on Credit Card Payments (Razorpay)

- Applicable **only** on Razorpay credit card transactions.
- **Not applicable** on UPI, Net Banking, Debit Cards, Wallets, or Smart Collect.
- Fee: **2% + GST**.
- Shown at Razorpay checkout after selecting payment method.
- Reflected in Seller Panel > Passbook.
- Monthly VAS invoice generated for the fee.

**Key dates:**
- Fee charging started: 7th July 2025.
- Passbook entries automated: 14th August 2025.
- RuPay credit card via UPI check live: 28th August 2025.

**Example:** If a seller recharges ₹1,00,000 via credit card, wallet is credited ₹97,640. A VAS entry of ₹2,360 is raised in real-time.

### Razorpay Transaction Status Actions

| Status | Meaning / Action |
|---|---|
| Authorised | Recharge has been done |
| Captured (over 1 hr) | Raise ticket to Tech Support to manually update payment |
| Captured (under 1 hr) | Ask seller to wait for cron cycle to complete |
| Failed | Inform seller: refund in 5–7 business days to source account |
| Created / Pending | Ask seller to wait 1 hour |

### Auto Wallet Transfer

Sellers can initiate wallet refund requests directly from the panel. The system auto-performs preliminary checks and calculates eligible refund amount. A support ticket is generated only if validation fails.

**Path:** Seller Panel > Help and Support > Create Ticket > Finance > Transfer Wallet Amount into My Bank Account

### Pre-Checks Before Wallet Balance Transfer

- No open shipments.
- Account status: Active.
- Bank / KYC details updated.
- COD unblocked.
- Wallet balance is positive (all linked accounts).

## SOP — Wallet Recharge

### Scenario 1 — Seller wants to know how to recharge

Guide seller to: **Seller Panel > Recharge Wallet**

Create an FCR ticket.

### Scenario 2 — Seller is unable to recharge

1. Request a screenshot within 48 hours of ticket creation.
2. Assign to **Customer Support Tech Desk**. TAT: 24 hours.

## SOP — Recharge Error / Issue

### System Check

1. Copy link from SPARK and paste in a new tab.
2. Confirm when the seller recharged and the amount.
3. Check the payment gateway (Razorpay / Paytm) for transaction details.

### Scenario 1 — No payment status visible on Razorpay

> I would like to request you to share the screenshot of the transaction ID on email. Once shared, please allow a maximum of two days for an update.

### Scenario 2 — Seller has shared the transaction ID screenshot

> I am escalating your issue. Please wait 1–2 working days.

## SOP — Wallet Balance Transfer to Bank

### Pre-Checks

Verify before raising to Finance Desk:
- No open shipments.
- Account status is Active.
- Bank / KYC details are updated.
- COD is unblocked.
- Wallet balance is positive.

If all checks pass → Raise to **Customer Support Finance Desk**. TAT: 1–2 working days.

If seller has coupon value in wallet:
> Sir / Ma'am, if you want to transfer your wallet amount to bank account, the coupon value will not be included in the transfer — only the wallet amount will be transferred.

### Nugget Ticket (NFCR)

| Field | Value |

## FAQ to Share with Sellers

**Why was the minimum recharge changed from ₹200 to ₹500?**

> We have observed that most sellers do a minimum of 10–15 shipments a month and must recharge repeatedly. To avoid the hassle of recurring recharges, we have changed the recharge value from ₹200 to ₹500. Your amount is safe in our highly secured wallet, just like any other transactional wallet. You can use this amount for future shipments.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Recharge Wallet

1. Go to **Seller Panel > Recharge Wallet** from the left menu.
2. Enter recharge amount (minimum ₹500).
3. Select payment method.
4. Complete payment.

> For credit card payments via Razorpay, a convenience fee of 2% + GST applies.

### Initiate Wallet Transfer to Bank

1. Go to **Help and Support > Create Ticket > Finance > Transfer Wallet Amount into My Bank Account**.
2. The system auto-checks eligibility and calculates the eligible transfer amount.
3. A ticket is auto-created if validation fails.

## Mobile App — How to Do

### Recharge Wallet (Mobile)

1. Go to the **Finance / Billing section** from the three-dots menu.
2. Tap **Recharge Wallet**.
3. Enter amount (minimum ₹500) and select payment method.
4. Complete payment.

### Check Wallet Balance (Mobile)

The wallet balance is visible on the **Home screen** of the Shiprocket app and in the Finance section.
