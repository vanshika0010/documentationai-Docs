## Product Info

### Same-Day Pickup Cut-Off

- **Standard:** Orders manifested before **11:00 AM** are scheduled for same-day pickup.
- **India Post:** Orders must be scheduled before **10:00 AM** for same-day pickup.
- **Return Orders:** Orders created before **1:00 PM** are picked up same day. After 1:00 PM, pickup is on the next day.

### Courier Pickup Attempts

The courier makes **3 attempts** to pick up your order. If unsuccessful after three attempts, no further attempts are made.

### Pickup ID

- A unique alphanumeric ID that groups shipments by pickup address, courier, and scheduled date.
- Used to escalate pickup delays.
- When all shipments from a pickup ID are cancelled, that pickup ID is removed from the system.

**Pickup ID Statuses:**
| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pickup Scheduled | Pickup has been scheduled |
| Pickup Completed | All shipments were picked up |
| Pickup Cancelled | All associated shipments are cancelled |
| Partially Completed | Only some shipments were picked up |
| Pickup Exception | Courier attempted pickup but could not complete it |

### NPR (Non-Pickup Reason)

When shipment pickup fails, seller receives 3 options via WhatsApp / IVR / SMS / Email:
1. **Re-attempt**
2. **Escalation** (with reason)
3. **Cancel Pickup Attempt** (order will not be cancelled)

**Communication Flow:**
1. Instant notification via WhatsApp and Email on NPR raise.
2. No response → IVR call after 60 minutes.
3. No IVR response → WhatsApp reminder after 30 minutes.
4. No WhatsApp response → 2nd IVR call after 60 minutes.
5. No response to 2nd IVR → data sent to Call Center after 30 minutes.

**Verified vs Non-Verified Pickup Failure:**
- **Verified Pickup Failure:** Seller refuses pickup after confirming through OTP.
- **Non-Verified Pickup Failure:** Seller refuses pickup without OTP confirmation.

### Instant Pickup — Shadowfax

| Order Created | Pickup Slot |
|---|---|
| 4:00 PM – 9:00 AM | 9 AM – 12 PM |
| 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM | 12 PM – 3 PM |
| 12:00 PM – 4:00 PM | 3 PM – 6 PM |

### Rocket Express Services

Shiprocket's own pickup fleet (**Delhi only**) for SMB sellers.
- FE scans shipment on spot — status changes to "Picked Up" immediately.
- Shipments are handed over to the originally assigned courier at the pickup hub.
- Seller can verify FE identity via the App (AWB number visible on FE app).
- For sellers outside Delhi — service is not available.

### Instant Pickup — Pitching

For urgent or irate sellers, suggest instant pickup with Shadowfax.

_Tag all cases where instant pickup is pitched with the "Instant Pickup" tag (FCR tagging)._

### Pickup Freight Refund Policy

| Delay | Refund Type | ICRM Path |
|---|---|---|
| 3 days | 20% partial freight refund | Shipment Detail > Decision Tree > Partial Freight Refund |
| 5 days | 100% full freight refund | Shipment Detail > Decision Tree > Full Freight Refund |

Inform seller of pickup TAT after initiating refund: if assigned before 11 AM, wait till day end; if after 11 AM, wait till next day end.

### Pickup Connection Dispute

If the FE has picked up the shipment but the status has not updated on the panel:

1. **Same day call:** Advise seller to wait till EOD.
2. **Status not updated by next day:** Request the seller to share within 48–72 hours:
   - Date of pickup
   - Total shipments picked up that day
   - All signed manifest copies for the courier
   - FE phone number
3. Inform seller: team will update within **7 working days**.
4. Send ACR mail from Nugget using template: _"Pickup connection issue"_.

**Nugget Tagging:**
- SR Issue Category: Shipment Pickup
- Sub Category: Pickup Miscellaneous
- FCR: NFCR

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Schedule Pickup for a Single Order

1. Go to **Orders > Ready to Ship tab**.
2. Find the order and click **Request Pickup / Schedule Pickup**.
3. In the pop-up, select today's date or a future date (up to 5 working days in advance).
4. Click **Schedule Pickup**.

### Schedule Pickup in Bulk

1. Go to **Orders > Ready to Ship tab**.
2. Select multiple orders.
3. Click **Select Bulk Action > Schedule Pickup**.
4. Manifest will be sent to your registered email.

### View Upcoming Pickups

1. Go to **Orders > Pickups & Manifests > Pickup IDs**.
2. See the Scheduled Pickup date under the Pickup status column.

### Escalate a Pickup Delay

1. Go to **Orders > Pickups & Manifests**.
2. Find the relevant Pickup ID.
3. Click **Create Ticket** next to that Pickup ID.
4. Select escalation reason from the dropdown.
5. Add remarks and click **Create Ticket**.
6. Update the mobile number where the support team will contact you.

> **Note:** Escalation based on Pickup ID only — Manifest ID can no longer be used for pickup escalations.

### Handle a Fake Pickup Attempt

1. Go to **Orders > Pickups & Manifests**.
2. Click **Create Ticket** next to the relevant Pickup ID.
3. Click **Proceed** in the pop-up.
4. Select escalation reason, upload pickup image, and click **Create Ticket**.
5. Shiprocket will revert within 24 hours.

### Reassign a Courier (to reschedule pickup)

1. Find the order under **Orders > Ready to Ship tab**.
2. Click **More (...) > Reassign Courier**.
3. Select a new courier partner.
4. Schedule pickup for the desired date.

### Block ODA and RTO Pincodes

**Settings > Courier Selection > Select Courier > Block Pincodes > Select option (Courier, Account, or Custom) > Submit**

- **ODA (Out of Delivery Area):** Remote pincodes where couriers refuse delivery.
- **RTO Pincodes:** Pincodes with high RTO rates.

## Mobile App — How to Do

### Schedule Pickup (Mobile)

1. From the bottom navigation, tap **Orders**.
2. Go to **Ready to Ship** tab.
3. Tap on your order and select **Schedule Pickup**.
4. Choose pickup date and confirm.

### Escalate Pickup Delay (Mobile)

1. From **Orders > Pickups & Manifests** tab.
2. Find the Pickup ID with the delay.
3. Tap **Create Ticket**.
4. Select escalation reason, add remarks, and submit.

> From the mobile app, pickup escalation is now based on **Pickup ID** rather than Manifest ID.
