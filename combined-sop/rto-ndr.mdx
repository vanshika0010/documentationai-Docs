## Product Info

### Key Terms

- **NDR (Non-Delivery Report):** A report raised when a delivery executive cannot deliver a shipment.
- **RTO (Return to Origin):** After 3 failed delivery attempts, the courier initiates a return to the seller's address.
- **BPR (Buyer Positive Response):** Buyer confirmation taken on call to reattempt delivery.
- **RTO-NDR:** When an RTO shipment cannot be delivered back to the seller.
- **RTO Acknowledged:** Seller has acknowledged receipt of the RTO shipment from the panel.
- **RTO EDD:** Estimated delivery date for an RTO shipment back to the seller. Visible in Orders > RTO tab.

### NDR — How It Works

When a delivery attempt fails:
1. NDR is raised with an "undelivered" reason.
2. Seller receives options via WhatsApp / panel / IVR to reattempt or RTO.
3. Courier makes up to 3 total delivery attempts.
4. After 3 failed attempts — shipment is marked **RTO Initiated**.

> Exception: If the NDR reason is "Customer Refused OTP Verified," the shipment is sent to RTO without waiting for 3 attempts.

### RTO-NDR Rules

- Seller must take action within **10 days** of RTO NDR being raised.
- If no action in 10 days — shipment is **disposed off**.
- Only **1 reattempt** allowed. If second attempt fails — shipment is disposed off.

**Email Notifications to Seller:**
- When RTO NDR is raised — email within 4 hours (includes all AWBs).
- Day 5 — reminder email.
- Day 9 — final reminder email.

### RTO Zone Delivery TAT

Return delivery TAT (excluding RTO Initiated date and Sundays):

| Zone | Description |
|---|---|
| A | Within City |
| B | Within State |
| C | Metro to Metro |
| D | Rest of India (excluding NE and J&K) |
| E | North East and J&K |

Standard RTO TAT: **15 working days** from date of RTO Initiated.

### Escalation Rules

- Seller can escalate after RTO EDD has been breached.
- Resolution TAT: **72 hours** from raised date.
- Re-escalation allowed within **48 hours** of escalation closure.
- Escalation auto-closes when status becomes: RTO NDR, RTO Delivered, Disposed, Destroyed, Lost, RTO Acknowledged, Put Away Done.

### Buyer Calling Rules

- No buyer calls can be arranged after **8:30 PM** as per TRAI guidelines.

## SOP — NDR

### System Check

1. Copy link from SPARK / Click on Internal ICRM.
2. View Details > NDR tab > Check NDR raised date and undelivered reason.
3. Check action taken by seller / buyer.
4. Check NDR communication (check Delivery Boost if enabled).

### NDR Seller Action

**Panel path:** Seller Panel > Shipments > Process NDR > Search AWB > Reattempt / RTO

TAT after BPR: 1–2 working days.

_If BPR is pending — assign case to LOPS with the **BPR Pending** tag._

### BPR (Buyer Positive Response) Checklist

When taking BPR on call:

1. Verify buyer's name and product details.
2. Validate non-delivery (NDR) reason from buyer.
3. Confirm if FE contacted or reached for delivery attempt (share attempt date and time).
4. Confirm if buyer is still ready to accept the **packed shipment** — deny if future delivery more than 2 days or open-box delivery is requested.
5. Confirm alternate mobile number and landmark.
6. Inform buyer they will receive a call from the delivery reattempt team.
7. BPR call control must stay with the advisor — limit seller interference.

### NDR CB Team Actions

1. Try BPR once. If no connectivity — raise NFCR to LOPS with **BPR Pending** tag.
2. If both seller and buyer are unreachable — raise NFCR for delivery reattempt and inform seller via email about non-connectivity.

### No Buyer Calls After 8:30 PM Script

> I understand your concern Sir / Ma'am. However, as per TRAI guidelines and our policy, buyer calls cannot be arranged after 8:30 PM to ensure privacy. But don't worry — I've escalated your concern to the courier team. Let's wait for their response by _(mention TAT)_.

## SOP — RTO Initiated (Out of TAT)

If the 15 working day TAT is breached:

1. Assign to LOPS — TAT: 1–2 working days.
2. Initiate **Soft Loss Refund** from ICRM: View Details > Shipment section > Soft Loss > Select reason > Submit.

> **Note:** Soft loss is temporary. Amount is debited from wallet once shipment is delivered. Cannot be transferred to bank.

## SOP — NDR Verification for RTO Shipment

1. Check shipment status in ICRM.
2. Check if NDR was sent and action taken by seller.
3. Inform seller: forwarding to team; verifying attempt from buyer. TAT: 1–2 days.

_Do not raise a ticket if the issue is pending more than 10 days._

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Process NDR (Reattempt or RTO)

1. Go to **Orders > NDR tab** (or Shipments > Process NDR).
2. Search by AWB.
3. Click **Act on Shipment**.
4. Select **Reattempt** (with updated address/number if needed) or **Mark RTO**.

### Mark an Order as RTO

1. Go to **Orders > NDR** from the left menu.
2. Click **View Details** next to the AWB.
3. Click **Mark RTO**.

### Take Action on RTO-NDR

1. Go to **Orders > RTO tab**.
2. Filter: **RTO NDR**.
3. Click **Reattempt** in the Action column.
4. Update phone number and/or address if needed (Delhivery, Ecom Express, Xpressbees only).
5. Select reattempt date.

### Raise RTO Delay Escalation

1. Go to **Orders > RTO tab**.
2. Find the shipment with breached RTO EDD.
3. Click **Create Ticket** button (visible only when EDD has passed).
4. Enter remarks and submit.

### Change Customer Phone Number (Post-Pickup)

1. Wait until the order reaches **NDR status**.
2. Go to **Orders > NDR panel**.
3. Click **More (...) > Act on Shipment > Update Details**.
4. Update the phone number.

## Mobile App — How to Do

### Process NDR on Mobile

1. Go to the three-dots menu at the bottom > **NDR section**.
2. Find the undelivered shipment.
3. Tap **Act on Shipment**.
4. Choose **Reattempt** or **Mark RTO**.
5. For reattempt: update alternate number, landmark, or preferred date.

### Check NDR Orders (Mobile)

1. Three-dots menu at bottom > **NDR**.
2. View all undelivered orders and their NDR reasons.
3. Tap **Track Order** for real-time status.

### Check RTO Orders (Mobile)

1. From bottom navigation, tap **Orders**.
2. Swipe to **RTO** tab.
3. View all RTO-status orders.
4. Tap **Acknowledge** once you have received the returned shipment.
