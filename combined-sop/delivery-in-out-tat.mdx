## Product Info

### Key Terms

- **EDD (Estimated Delivery Date):** The date by which the shipment is expected to be delivered. Visible on the panel only if pickup has been scheduled.
- **Updated EDD:** A revised delivery estimate provided by the courier if the original EDD is breached.
- **Suppress EDD:** A flag in ICRM — if suppressed, the EDD shown to the seller may not be accurate.
- **Courier Under Stress:** A pincode marked as "Stressed" in the courier name during order creation. Deliveries to these pincodes may be impacted.

### Delivery Escalation — Seller Panel

Sellers can escalate directly from the panel when the EDD is breached:

**Path:** Seller Panel > Shipments > Track > Paste AWB > Escalate (under Expedite Delivery)

Or via:

**Path:** Orders > In-Transit tab > Bulk Escalate (for multiple delayed orders)

### Checking Order Statuses

| Status | Filter Path |

## SOP — Delivery In TAT and Out of TAT

### System Check

1. Copy link from SPARK and paste in a new tab / Click on Internal ICRM.
2. View Details > Shipment section.
3. Check **Suppress EDD**.
4. Check **SR Tracking**.
5. Check **First EDD** and **Updated EDD**.
6. Check **Courier Remarks**.

### Delivery In-TAT

Inform the seller of the EDD (day end as per the EDD).

If shipment is delayed within TAT — advise seller to escalate from panel:

**Path:** Seller Panel > Shipments > Track > Paste AWB > Escalate (under Expedite Delivery)

### Tracking Status Mismatch

If the delivery location differs from the tracking update:

1. Confirm with seller — does he want a delivery attempt or RTO?
2. Assign to LOPS per seller VOC.
3. TAT: 1–2 working days.

## How to Do on Seller Panel

### Track a Shipment

1. Go to **Orders** from the left menu.
2. Use the search bar at the top to search by AWB number.
3. Check the current status under the **Status** column.

Or:

1. Go to **Orders > In-Transit tab**.
2. Click the **Track** button next to any order.

### Check EDD for a Shipment

1. Go to **Orders** from the left menu.
2. Search by AWB or Order ID.
3. EDD is shown under the **Status** column.

> EDD is only visible if pickup has been scheduled.

### Raise a Delivery Delay Complaint

1. Go to **Orders** from the left menu.
2. Search for the AWB.
3. Click the **Create Ticket** button.
4. Enter your delivery delay remarks in the box.
5. Click **Create Ticket** to submit.

> This option is only available if the order has crossed the EDD.

### Raise a Delivery Dispute (DNR / Wrong / Damaged / Empty)

1. Go to **Orders** from the left menu.
2. Search for the AWB.
3. Click **More (...) > Create Ticket**.
4. Select **Order not delivered** OR **Wrong/Damaged/Partial/Empty Package Delivered**.
5. Fill in required details and upload proof images/videos.
6. Click **Submit**.

> Disputes must be raised **within 72 hours** of delivery. A dispute can be raised a maximum of two times. Resolution TAT: 10 business days.

### Raise RTO Delay Escalation

1. Go to **Orders > RTO tab**.
2. Find the shipment with breached RTO EDD.
3. Click **Create Ticket** (appears when RTO EDD has passed).
4. Enter remarks and click **Create Ticket**.

> Re-escalation allowed within 48 hours of escalation closure.

### Download POD (Proof of Delivery)

POD is available for: Delhivery, Bluedart, Shadowfax, Ekart, XpressBees, and DTDC — **forward delivery only**.

1. Go to **Orders > Delivered tab**.
2. Click **More (...) > Download POD**.

> POD is available **24 hours after delivery**.

## Mobile App — How to Do

### Track a Shipment (Mobile)

1. From the bottom navigation bar, tap **Orders**.
2. Go to **In Transit** tab.
3. Search by AWB or tap **Track Order** next to the shipment.

### Raise a Delivery Escalation (Mobile)

1. Go to **Orders > In-Transit tab**.
2. Find the delayed order.
3. Tap **Escalate** to raise the issue.
