## Inbound Call Script

### Opening

Good morning / afternoon / evening. Welcome to Shiprocket. My name is _______. How may I assist you?

**Acknowledgement (English):** Surely, I will assist you regarding your concern — [mention seller's concern].

**Acknowledgement (Hindi):** Nishchint rheye, mei apki sahayta krne ki puri koshish krunga / krungi.

### Hold Script

May I place your call on hold for 60 seconds so that I can give you better information?

**Hindi:** Behtar jaankari ke liye kya mei apki call 60 seconds ke liye hold pr rkh skta / skti hu?

### Un-hold Script

Thank you for being on hold!

_If hold exceeds 60 seconds:_ I am sorry to keep you waiting for such a long time.

**Hindi:** Hold par bane rhne ke liye dhanywaad! _(If hold exceeds 60 seconds)_ Lambe hold ke liye maafi chahunga.

### Further Assistance

Is there anything else I may assist you with regarding Shiprocket?

**Hindi:** Kya mei aapki Shiprocket ke regarding kuch or sahyta kar sakta / sakti hu?

### CSAT Script

If you are satisfied with the information provided, please share your valuable feedback over the email you will receive after this call.

**Hindi:** Agar aap meri di gyi jankari se santusht hei, toh kripya apna valuable feedback is call ke baad bheji gyi email par de sakte hei.

### Google Review Script

If you are satisfied with the information provided, please share your valuable review on the Shiprocket website. You will receive the link after this call on your registered email ID.

### Closing

Thank you for calling Shiprocket. Have a good day.

**Hindi:** Shiprocket mei call krne ke liye apka dhanywaad! Apka din shubh ho!

## CSAT and IVR Rating

### CSAT Script — Voice

Sir / Ma'am, I hope you are satisfied with the resolution provided to you.

_If seller says yes:_ Sir / Ma'am, after this call you will receive a survey message on your registered email ID. Request you to kindly share your valuable feedback for our conversation.

### CSAT Script — Chat

Sir / Ma'am, I hope you are satisfied with the resolution provided to you.

_If seller says yes:_ Sir / Ma'am, post this chat you will receive a survey message on your registered email ID. Request you to kindly share your valuable feedback for this chat.

### Rating IVR Scale

| Rating | Result |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | DSAT |
| 3 | Neutral |
| 4–5 | CSAT |

**Closing statement before IVR transfer:**

> I will now transfer the call to the IVR for a quick survey. Thank you for calling Shiprocket. Have a nice day.

## Apology, Empathy, and Power Statements

### Apology Statements

- I apologize for the inconvenience that you are facing.
- Apologies for the trouble; our team is looking into it.
- I am extremely sorry for the inconvenience.
- I am extremely sorry about this situation.
- I'm sorry for the trouble that you have faced.

### Empathy Statements

- I understand that this situation is difficult, but rest assured I will find a solution for you.
- I do understand your situation. I will surely help you with this.
- I understand sir — this is wrong and this must have given an impact to the business.
- I would feel the same if this happened to me.
- Let me check what best I can do for you.

### Power Statements

**For NDR Desk:**
> Don't worry Sir / Ma'am, I will take up your issue with the courier and try to get it resolved at the earliest. We will update you on the status of the case.

**For Delivery Dispute Desk (evidence required):**
> To take up the dispute with the courier, I need your assistance in providing the required _(name the requirement)_ evidence.

**General Power Lines:**
- I understand that this situation is difficult, but don't worry Sir / Ma'am, I will get this resolved for you.
- I do understand your situation. Let me see how I can help you here.
- I understand — this must have given an impact to the business.
- Don't worry, I will try my best to fix this for you.
- Let me check what best I can do for you — allow me once.
- I understand this is troubling you but don't worry, I am here to assist you every step of the way.
- I will put my best efforts to help you.
